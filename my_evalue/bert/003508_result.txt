customs officials have seized what is being described as a ' zoo in a suitcase ' from a woman accused of smuggling 108 exotic animals in russia . two baby crocodiles died on the journey from jakarta to moscow and all the survivors appeared terrified when they were found after the 6,515-mile ordeal . horrendous treatment : the menagerie came to light when customs officials at domodedovo airport electronically scanned a 27kg bag belonging to a female passenger who had flown from indonesia via qatar . after being impounded , the hungry and exhausted creatures were handed to employees of moscow 's ' exotic park ' where they will be cared for until their future is decided .
