harry redknapp has revealed he was attacked with coins and verbally abused by arsenal fans as he left the emirates stadium after the gunners ' 4-1 win over liverpool . currently out of work after leaving his position at queens park rangers , the former tottenham boss was at the emirates as a spectator on april 4 when some fans turned against him . redknapp claims he had earlier signed autographs and posed for pictures with friendly supporters , but when he was stuck in traffic after the game , a group of supporters began abusing him .
