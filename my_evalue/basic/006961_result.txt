the remarkable images were shot by photographer victor albert prout , who developed the negatives in an improvised dark room on board his punt which he sailed along the river thames . prout travelled along the river thames in a makeshift boat where he installed a dark room ( centre , front ) , capturing and developing his images on site , like this one of windsor castle ( top right ) . as an artist and photographer was renowned for his panoramic pictures , captured along the stretch of the river thames - featuring images from the houses of parliament to magdalen bridge in oxford .
