homeless people in the bay area are being handed free smartphones by multi-billion dollar tech companies in the hope that it will get them off the streets . a group called community technology alliance is giving away free google handsets which come loaded with apps that help the homeless find shelters , soup kitchens , and warn of severe weather . holly leonard ( left ) , who was homeless and had spent time in prison , now has a house after finding an advert on craigslist using a google phone she was given to allow her to get online .
