it is the age old debate but with a modern twist : which premier league club is the most popular according to twitter followers . arsenal have strong support in london and the south east and actually have more followers in the backyards of rivals chelsea and tottenham . chelsea have strong support in the western isles , while clubs such as newcastle , sunderland , hull city , burnley , stoke city , leicester city , aston villa , swansea and southampton dominate their local areas .
