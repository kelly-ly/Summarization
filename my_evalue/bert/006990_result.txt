vladmir lenin may have been dead for 90 years , but his corpse looks better than the day he passed . the bolshevik leader 's remains were embalmed on stalin 's orders when he died aged 53 in 1924 and kept on display in moscow apart from a period in the second world war . the revolutionary 's body is on public display in a mausoleum on moscow 's red square more than two decades after the break-up of the former ussr . born in simbirsk , he was expelled from university for his radical policies while studying law .
