architect and photographer yener torun sought to challenge the one dimensional view of his changing city , and captures the new , minimalistic buildings that add a splash of colour to istanbul . yener spends his time wandering around the city looking for bold , geometric buildings to photograph , and documents his work to his instagram , which has over 50,000 followers . the photographer , who has lived in istanbul for 14 years , aims to show a clean minimalistic view of the city , as it contrasts with the ornate , ottoman architecture .
