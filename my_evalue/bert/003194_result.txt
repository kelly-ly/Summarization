the duke of kent has been spotted leaving hospital with a walking stick after suffering a dislocated hip during an easter trip to the royal family 's balmoral estate . the duke is the son of prince george , the younger brother of king george vi and king edward viii . it is not known how the duke injured himself but he was taken to hospital from balmoral castle in royal deeside , aberdeenshire , which has been one of the residences for members of the royal family since 1852 .
