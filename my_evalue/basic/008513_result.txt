nick abendanon was perhaps the only happy englishman at stade marcel michelin on saturday night and , after leading northampton a merry dance , he revealed faint but lingering world cup hopes . clermont auvergne’s former bath full back ran amok as the premiership champions were thrashed 37-5 — scoring one try from long range and having a crucial hand in two others , to claim the man-of-the-match award . abendanon won the last of his two caps for england back in 2007 , but he was still on the coaches’ radar until his move across the channel last summer and , after a supreme display of elusive running , he admitted he has not given up on the slim possibility of a test recall .
