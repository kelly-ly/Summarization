as the champions league starters took a break it was business as usual for the rest of the real madrid squad on wednesday as they returned to training a day after the last eight clash with rivals atletico . there was no sign of the likes of cristiano ronaldo , gareth bale and james rodriguez - all of who played 90 minutes in the 0-0 draw at vicente calderon on tuesday - as the fringe players took part in a session at the valdebebas complex . isco and alvaro arbeloa , second-half substitutes during the first leg against atletico , were present alongside regular first team players pepe and sami khedira , who were on the bench on tuesday .
