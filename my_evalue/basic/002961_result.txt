an investigation spanning almost three years into the yearly cost of gun violence covered by u.s. taxpayers has determined the figure to be over $ 229 billion . the new study , by independent political magazine mother jones , was spawned by the mass shooting in aurora , colorado , in july 2012 during a midnight screening of the dark knight rises , in which 12 people were killed and 70 injured . the magazine says it set out to determine the amount of money the survivors and victims ' families from that one incident would need , based on medical expenses , lost wages , court costs , therapy and impact on quality of life .
