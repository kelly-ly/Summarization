 doctors in kurdistan are breaking the law by performing abortions on young yazidi girls who have been released after being held as sex slaves by isis fighters . hundreds of yazidi girls and women , some as young as eight , were kidnapped by isis last year , and have since been held as sex slaves in the islamic state . the young girls , some barely teenagers , are some of around 40,000 people kidnapped at gunpoint when islamic state fighters attacked yazidi villages last summer .
