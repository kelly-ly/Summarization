the letter , dated may 7 , 1912 , was sent from white star lines to christopher moody , the brother of 24-year-old officer james moody , who died after the titanic hit an iceberg on its maiden voyage . bosses from ismay imrie & co , co-owners of white star lines , wrote this letter to the family of officer james moody who died on board the titanic , telling them his body could be returned - but only for a price . the letter , from bosses at ismay imrie & co , the parent company of white star line , read : ' we have your further letter of the 6th instant , and while we will be prepared to transport the remains of your brother across the atlantic to either liverpool or southampton we regret that it is not possible for us to do any more .
