a british holidaymaker had to be forcibly removed from a manchester-bound plane in egypt after he allegedly threatened to kill other passengers . the plane was minutes away from take-off at the airport in the red sea city of hurghada when an unruly passenger began causing problems near the back of the plane , a witness said . tony penner , a passenger on the plane , told the manchester evening news that the disruptive man punched a bus door and uttered threats as travellers were being driven to the plane on friday night . he told the manchester evening news : ‘ as soon as the steps went down an officer with a pistol came on board . ‘
