luke munro , 19 , and dylon thompson , 21 , burst into the londis shop in cleveleys near blackpool in october last year while the owner , his wife , and their toddler were behind the counter . when the mother and father refused to hand over cash , munro and thompson threw sweets at them before storming out empty handed . at that point , cctv footage shows munro and thompson began throwing handfuls of sweets at the couple behind the counter , before fleeing empty handed .
