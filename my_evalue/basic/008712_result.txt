delphi automotive has completed the longest automated drive in the us , travelling from san francisco to new york in the first coast-to-coast trip ever attempted by an automated vehicle . along the way , the vehicle encountered complex driving situations such as traffic circles , construction zones , bridges , tunnels , aggressive drivers and a variety of weather conditions . jeff owens , delphi 's chief technology officer , sees the automated driver assistance systems in the audi as building blocks on the road to fully self-driving cars in the next decade .
