eden hazard is reaching the incredible standards of lionel messi and cristiano ronaldo with his ‘ dancing feet’ , says graeme souness . the chelsea star reached 100 premier league appearances for the blues at qpr on sunday and has been lauded for his fine performances for jose mourinho’s side this season . sky sports pundit souness believes hazard , who has 35 goals and 25 assists in his century of premier league games , will be named the pfa player of the year .
