a magnitude 3.1 earthquake caused a sharp jolt to be felt across a well-populated area just north of los angeles . the us geological survey said in a preliminary report that the quake with a depth of about five miles happened just before 9pm last night , according to the associated press . it was centered about four miles north of san fernando near interstate 210 . the quake came three days after a magnitude 3.5 earthquake hit the west los angeles neighborhood of baldwin hills .
