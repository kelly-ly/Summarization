a pregnant mother with a rare form of dwarfism has revealed the physical challenges of raising her three-year-old son - who is more than half her size - while preparing for the arrival of her second child . tiffanie didonato , from swansboro , north carolina , was born with diastrophic dysplasia and underwent numerous limb-lengthening surgeries as a child so she would grow to be 4'10 tall , instead of her previous height of 3'8 , which is how tall her son titan currently is . helping hand : tiffanie didonato , who has diastrophic dysplasia , a rare form of dwarfism , can be seen grocery shopping with her three-year-old son titan , who is more than half her size , but always willing to assist her .
