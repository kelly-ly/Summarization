abc is filming a pilot episode of the muppet show , in the hopes of bringing the beloved variety show back to the small screen . the network has asked big bang theory co-creator bill prady to mastermind the revival , which would see the return of kermit the frog , miss piggy , fozzie bear and other old favorites . the episode will start with muppets stalwarts including kermit , fozzie and gonzo gathering at abc studios in the hope of getting the new show off the ground .
