a japan railway maglev train hit 603 kilometers per hour ( 374 miles per hour ) on an experimental track in yamanashi tuesday , setting a decisive new world record . the train broke its own record from last thursday , when it ran at 590 kilometers per hour ( 366 miles per hour ) on a test track . that beat the old record of 581 kilometers per hour ( 361 miles per hour ) , which was set in 2003 during another japanese maglev test .
