looking after someone with dementia can stretch people to their limits , and there are many in this situation . the early stages of the disease bring changes that may be so subtle that some friends and acquaintances are unaware that there is anything wrong - and this stage can continue for many years . in the early stages , aim to establish fixed times of day for the following - it may help to write this on a board that 's kept next to a calendar ( cross it off to show what day it is ) and a clock , so that they know what to expect when .
