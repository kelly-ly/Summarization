brendan rodgers admitted liverpool’s big-game mentality must improve after his hopes of winning the fa cup were left in tatters . the liverpool manager had no complaints about their 2-1 defeat by aston villa — their first loss in an fa cup semi-final since 1990 — and accepted his side have to do better in the most important games . even though they took the lead with a well-worked goal by philippe coutinho ( right ) in the 30th minute , liverpool never gave the impression they would give steven gerrard a dream date in the final on his 35th birthday and their lame performance left rodgers shattered .
