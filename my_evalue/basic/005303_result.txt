motherwell have vowed to go to battle with the spfl over the issue of free entry to the premiership play-offs for season-ticket holders . under existing rules , the spfl are entitled to take 50 per cent of the gate proceeds from the six play-off ties for redistribution among the clubs . expressing unease among the play-off finalists that the spfl might be using the ties to milk supporters , motherwell general manager alan burrows told sportsmail his club will make a stand if they finish in the premiership play-off place .
