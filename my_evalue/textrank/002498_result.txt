the obama administration moved on two fronts today to advance its nuclear diplomacy with iran , with talks between top u.s. and iranian diplomats at a united nations conference in new york and an aggressive effort to sell the emerging deal to skeptical american lawmakers and constituencies in washington . negotiator wendy sherman told a conference of reform jews that diplomatic collapse would leave iran perilously close to nuclear weapons capacity . particularly problematic for president barack obama and his team are how quickly sanctions would be eased for tehran in exchange for nuclear concessions , and how to snap economic restrictions back into place quickly if iran is caught cheating .
