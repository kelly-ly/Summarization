a high school student in china has spent three years literally shouldering the responsibility of ensuring that his physically disabled best friend gets to all his classes . 18-year-old xie xu ( left ) carries 19-year-old classmate zhang chi ( right ) to school each day . the story of the two students is so inspiring and touching . the teenagers live in dorms close by to the school , and each day xie carries zhang from their rooms to the school , and back again , and helps him with daily tasks such as getting meals and washing clothes .
