an eight-year-old boy who was made an outcast in his village and described by his own family as a ' ticking time bomb ' because he has hiv has been given a home at last . in a case that was condemned around the world , luo kun kun was expelled from his village when locals - including his own grandfather - signed a petition to banish him to ' protect villagers ' health ' . the sichuan government contacted the school in linfen when kun kun 's plight made worldwide headlines . kun kun did not say a word when he arrived at the school , according to the headteacher , but can study and talk like a normal student now .
