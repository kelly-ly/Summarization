gabriel ng spent countless hours filming thailand’s picturesque coastline , religious sites and scenes of everyday life using a camera attached to a remote control drone . gabriel , who co-owns a video production company with a fellow cinematographer , filmed most of the scenes on the island of ko samui , in the gulf of thailand , during a solo trip to visit family . using a drone that retails from £500 , gabriel’s high definition video shows the sun peeking over mountaintops , fishing boats on the calm sea and waterfalls .
