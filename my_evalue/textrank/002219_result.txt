child star : shirley temple made her name as the child actor who cheered america through the depression but in later years she was a committed republican who wanted to serve her country . public roles : she was initially used to fame as the star of bright eyes , curly top and heidi , but shirley temple turned to public service and was us ambassador to ghana in 1974-1976 and czechoslovakia 1989-1992 . political : richard nixon had known shirley temple long before he was president .
