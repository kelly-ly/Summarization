no blame : baroness hale of richmond said that she wanted to see the bitterness taken out of most matrimonial disputes . divorcing couples currently have to cite one of five reasons : adultery ; unreasonable behaviour ; desertion for two or more years ; two years’ separation with consent ; or five years’ separation without consent . lady hale , 70 , was behind the move for ‘ no fault’ divorce in england and wales 20 years ago .
