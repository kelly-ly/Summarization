hundreds of seals are being killed in british waters in order to protect stocks of salmon and other fish which are destined for supermarket shelves . the practice of seal clubbing in canada has long been regarded with revulsion around the world - but many britons may be unaware that the animals are also being killed in the uk . some campaigners suggest that the real number of dead seals is much higher , as the official figure only includes those which were recorded to the authorities by people who had previously obtained licences .
