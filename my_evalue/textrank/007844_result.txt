germany 's angelique kerber came from a set down to outmuscle caroline wozniacki 3-6 , 6-1 , 7-5 on sunday and win the stuttgart grand prix for her second title of the season . wozniacki saved three match points before the 27-year-old kerber kept her cool to convert her fourth chance for her second title in a month after charleston . kerber comes forward to play the forehand before going on to claim victory on home soil .
