telecom watchdogs are to stop a rip-off that allows mobile phone firms to charge customers to call supposedly free 0800 numbers . currently mobile phone companies charge customers up to 20p a minute to dial what are free numbers from a landline phone . end call : charges of up to 20p a minute being charged by operators for free 0800 numbers will end from july 1 under proposals published by the telecoms watchdog , ofcom , today .
