( cnn ) novak djokovic extended his current winning streak to 17 matches after beating thomas berdych 7-5 , 4-6 , 6-3 in the rain-interrupted final of the monte carlo masters . djokovic edged a tight first set before losing the second after the czech sixth seed took advantage of the short rain delay and came back strongly . `` it was a tough match , a particular match , '' said djokovic after winning his 52nd career title , and his second monte carlo masters championship .
