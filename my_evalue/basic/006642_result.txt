google wallet says it has changed its policy when storing users ' funds as they will now be federally-insured ( file photo ) . google confirmed to yahoo finance in a statement that its current policy changed - meaning the company will store the balances for users of the mobile transfer service ( similar to paypal and venmo ) in multiple federally-insured banking institutions . this is good news for people who place large amounts of money in their wallet balance because the federal deposit insurance corporation insures funds for banking institutions up to $ 250,000 .
