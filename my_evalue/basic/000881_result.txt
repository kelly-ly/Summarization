the kentucky wildcats ' bid for perfection ended saturday night at the hands of the wisconsin badgers , who rallied for a tough 71-64 victory in the ncaa men 's basketball final four at indianapolis . national player of the year finalist frank kaminsky led wisconsin with 20 points and 11 rebounds . freshman justise winslow led duke with 19 points while national freshman of the year jahlil okafor had 18 points , 10 of which came in the first half .
