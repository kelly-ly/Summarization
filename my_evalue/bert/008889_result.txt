we all know the time-consuming hassle of having to hoover the house , so it is perhaps no surprise to find that sailors on board this russian aircraft carrier also got bored of sweeping the decks . the crew of the admiral kuznetsov , russia 's largest warship , came up with the novel idea to help keep the deck clear of debris which poses a major risk to jet planes taking off and landing . usually crew members are forced to go around manually checking for stray objects , using sweeper vehicles to help tidy them up . but with 14,700sq ft of deck space to check and recheck every single day , the crew of the kuznetsov decided to do away with that bother , and took a more direct approach .
