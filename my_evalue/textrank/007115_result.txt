liverpool starlet joao teixeira has been ruled out for the rest of the season after suffering a broken leg during his loan spell at brighton . the 22-year-old , who has been in impressive form since sealing a move to the south coast in august , sustained the injury during brighton 's goalless draw with huddersfield on tuesday night . teixeira was taken to the royal sussex county hospital in brighton after he was carried off on a stretcher in the 80th minute during the championship encounter .
