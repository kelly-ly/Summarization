a new york judge has granted two chimpanzees held at a research lab the same rights as human prisoners , after a two year legal battle by an animal rights organization . a representative of the long island university have been ordered to appear in court to respond to a petition by the nonhuman rights project that chimps hercules and leo are ' unlawfully detained . ' making history : the two chimpanzees , living in a lab at stony brook university , long island , ( not pictured ) are the first animals to be granted the same rights as human prisoners .
