washington believes that the ‘ special relationship’ between britain and the us is over , according to a secret briefing document seen by the mail on sunday . dated april 2015 and drawn up to brief the senate and house of representatives on the impact of britain’s general election , the memo also warns that the uk faces turmoil if there is a hung parliament . the document – prepared by the congressional research service , an in-house intelligence body that gives confidential analysis to legislators – states that while britain and the us are likely to ‘ remain key economic partners’ , a ‘ reassessment of the special relationship may be in order… because its geopolitical setting has been changing’ .
