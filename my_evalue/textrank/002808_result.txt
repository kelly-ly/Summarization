president barack obama declared the threat of cyber attacks by foreign agents a ' national emergency ' as he unveiled plans to impose sanctions on hackers in the wake of an epidemic of attacks against american networks . obama said cyber attacks - many which originate from overseas - ' pose one of the most serious economic and national security challenges to the united states ' . lisa monaco , the president 's assistant for homeland security and counterterrorism , wrote in a blog : ' in just the past year , we have seen a significant increase in the frequency , scale , and sophistication of cyber incidents targeting the american people , including everything from large data breaches and significant intrusions to destructive and coercive cyber attacks intended to influence the way ordinary americans exercise their constitutional rights . ' in many cases , these threats stem from actors overseas using malicious cyber activities to inflict harm on americans without ever leaving their desks . '
