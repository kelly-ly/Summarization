new york is set to officially break ground this week on a ferris wheel that could become the tallest in the world once it is completed in early 2017 . ticket prices will range from an estimated $ 25 to $ 30 ( £17 to £20 ) as new york wheel llc , the company behind the ambitious project , hopes it will join the statue of liberty , empire state building and times square as one of new york’s must-visit attractions . wil armstrong , president of starneth llc , told mashable that the dubai eye is ahead , but ‘ anything could happen to delay it’ and give new york wheel the lead .
