manchester city 's youngsters finished off a job well done against leicester city , adding to their 3-0 first-leg lead with two late goals from isaac buckley and brandon barker at the king power stadium . layton ndukwu had given leicester hope of clawing back the advantage with 20 minutes to go but substitute buckley and barker struck to kill that dream off and set up a final against chelsea later this month . brandon barker ( right ) scored city 's second goal of the evening as they made it to a first final in seven years .
