the large hadron collider ( lhc ) , a particle accelerator and the largest machine in the world , is ready for action following a two-year shutdown . after problems that delayed the restart in march , scientists at the european organization for nuclear research ( cern ) completed final tests , enabling the first beams to start circulating sunday inside the lhc 's 17 mile ( 27 km ) ring . `` operating accelerators for the benefit of the physics community is what cern 's here for , '' cern director-general rolf heuer said on the organization 's website .
