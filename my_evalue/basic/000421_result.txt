( the hollywood reporter ) stan freberg , whose freewheeling comic career in advertising garnered him worldwide acclaim and whose satirical entertainments abounded on tv , the radio and on records , has died . freberg died of natural causes at a santa monica hospital , his son and daughter , donavan and donna freberg , confirmed to the hollywood reporter . three years later , he presented `` the stan freberg show '' on cbs radio , where he regularly mocked commercials by advertising bogus products .
