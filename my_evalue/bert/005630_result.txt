paris saint-germain have held initial discussions over a possible move for dinamo kiev winger andriy yarmolenko . psg are weighing up options with manchester united refusing to budge on angel di maria . yarmolenko is represented by mino raiola who also looks after zlatan ibrahimovic , romelu lukaku , mario balotelli and another psg target paul pogba of juventus . yarmolenko has been in superb form for his club this season , scoring five goals in just 10 games .
