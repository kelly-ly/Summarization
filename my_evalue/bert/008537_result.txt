a 13-year-old indian girl faced with an arranged marriage has written a letter to her teacher begging her to stop the wedding ( stock image above ) . he told the hindustan times that it was difficult to find a suitable match for a grown-up girl . the child marriage restraint act , passed in india during british rule in 1929 , specified that a girl must be 18 and a boy 21 before they can wed . meanwhile , in africa , 42 per cent of girls were married before turning 18 compared to 29 per cent in latin america and the caribbean .
