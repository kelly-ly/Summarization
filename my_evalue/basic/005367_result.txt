a host of clubs are tracking aston villa’s young central defender kevin toner as his contract nears its end . norwich , leicester , newcastle and stoke have watched toner , one of villa’s most promising young players who captains their under-21 team aged just 18 . tim sherwood is keen to develop villa’s young talent and toner has been among a select few to train with the first team , alongside the likes of lewis kinsella and rushian hepburn-murphy .
