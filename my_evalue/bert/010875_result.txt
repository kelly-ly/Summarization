it 's a dog-eat-dog world out there , as french restaurateurs have discovered . a report to the french government is urging eateries and customers to embrace ' le doggy bag ' in a bid to reduce the staggering amount of food thrown away each year . socialist mp guillaume garot , the author of the study , said while the popular us practice was previously ' unthinkable ' for snooty diners and chefs , a change in attitudes could save france up to €20 billion ( £14 billion ) a year . a un report found 1.3 billion tons of food was wasted globally each year , the equivalent of a third of food produced .
