( cnn ) kayahan , one of turkey 's best-loved singers and songwriters , died of cancer friday at the age of 66 . the performer , who was also an accomplished guitarist , was first diagnosed with cancer in 1990 , the year he competed in the eurovision song contest , and the year before he released the album that ignited his career . `` we are in grief over losing kayahan , who contributed to turkish music with countless compositions and marked a generation with his songs , '' prime minister ahmet davutoglu tweeted .
