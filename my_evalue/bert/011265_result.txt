a mother whose baby nearly died after he was born severely premature has urged other women to be aware of the infection that triggered her early labour . mrs butler , a marketing consultant from milton keynes , later discovered her premature labour had been triggered by a common infection called bacterial vaginosis ( bv ) . bv affects one in three women , making it twice as common as thrush . if untreated , it can lead to fertility problems and increased risk of contracting stis such as gonorrhoea and chlamydia .
