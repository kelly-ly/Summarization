identical twins start life with the same genes because they come from the same fertilised egg which divides into two embryos . using a technique called high resolution melt curve analysis - or hrma - researchers can now identify the difference between each twin . in 2013 , 22-year-old twins mohammed and aftab asghar were both charged with raping a 17-year-old woman in berkshire because the police were unsure which one dna evidence came from .
