discount store aldi’s astonishing success story continued yesterday as it overtook upmarket rival waitrose to become the sixth biggest supermarket in britain . between january and the end of march , aldi sales grew by 17 per cent from the same time a year earlier . aldi has 560 stores and hopes to double in size by 2022 . but fraser mckevitt , at kantar worldpanel , said the success of aldi and lidl meant the entire structure of the ‘ supermarket landscape’ was changing .
