dongguan , china ( cnn ) for a decade , the new south china mall -- the biggest shopping mall in the world -- has been an embarrassment for its owners and china . the mall spans five million square feet of shopping area , making it the largest in the world in terms of leasable space -- more than twice the size of mall of america , the biggest shopping center in the united states . in 2007 , the mall changed name from `` south china mall '' to `` new south china mall , living city '' and a revitalization plan was drawn up by current owners the founders group , a conglomerate set up by peking university , and its subsidiary pku founder .
