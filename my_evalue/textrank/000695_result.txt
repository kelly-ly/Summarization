( cnn ) wikileaks founder julian assange has agreed to be interviewed by swedish prosecutors in london , his lawyer in sweden told cnn . according to the lawyer , thomas olsson , swedish prosecutors will now have to reach out to british and ecuadorian authorities to request permission to conduct the interview at the embassy . however , some of the alleged crimes will be subject to a statute of limitations in august 2015 , according to a statement from marianne ny , the director of public prosecutions .
