former world champion ken doherty is on the brink of exiting the betfred world snooker championships in the final qualifying round . the 45-year-old faces an uphill battle to avoid becoming the third crucible winner to crash out this week , with peter ebdon and steve davis having lost in the second round . doherty enjoys a pint of guinness after winning the world championships in 1997 . elsewhere , former world championship runner-up matthew stevens leads joe swail 5-4 as he bids for a first crucible appearance since 2013 .
