second-half goals by mohamed yattara and alexandre lacazette helped olympique lyonnais go top of ligue 1 with a 2-0 home win against bastia on wednesday . alexandre lacazette and mohamed yattara were both on the score sheet as lyon beat bastia . lyon dominated throughout but were made to wait by bastia , who were reeling from their 4-0 defeat in the league cup final against psg last saturday .
