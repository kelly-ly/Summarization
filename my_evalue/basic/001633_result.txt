celebrities and music lovers gathered on friday in the southern california desert for the beginning of the annual coachella valley music and arts festival - the first big arts and music festival of the summer season . as in previous years , the 16th edition has attracted large crowds as festival goers danced around in the sun while performers including the headlining act ac/dc was scheduled to take the stage as well as musicians such as azealia banks , tame impala , alabama shakes and interpol . festival goers dance in the sahara tent during day one of the 2015 coachella valley music and arts festival at the empire polo club on friday .
