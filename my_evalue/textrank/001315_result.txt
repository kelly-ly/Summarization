an american gold mining company has paid what is beleived to be a six-figure compensation to 11 women after they were raped by the firm’s security guards and police patrolling porgera gold mine in papua new guinea . barrick gold , which boasts that it is the world’s leading gold mining company , reached the out-of-court settlement when the women threatened to file a lawsuit in the us . the abc said allegations of brutality had surrounded the mine even before barrick gold took it over in 2006 .
