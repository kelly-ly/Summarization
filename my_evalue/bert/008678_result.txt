the wedding of the year in scotland takes place on saturday when british no 1 and two-time grand slam champion andy murray marries kim sears , his girlfriend of almost 10 years , in his hometown of dunblane . murray and sears laugh with television host jimmy fallon before an appearance on the show following his us open victory . murray leans over to kiss sears after becoming the first british man in 77 years to win the men 's singles title at wimbledon in july 2013 . murray and sears pose with the famous trophy during the wimbledon champion 's dinner at a hotel in park lane later that evening .
