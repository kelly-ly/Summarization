gruesome death rituals performed 1,200 years ago have been uncovered in dozens of tombs in peru 's cotahuasi valley . archaeologists have so far opened up seven of the tombs , recovering 171 broken mummies from the ancient ceremonial site known as tenahaha . the pottery at the site is also decorated with what looks like depictions of people smiling . there is also evidence that the rituals went on for days at a time , with parties being held in the valley below the tombs .
