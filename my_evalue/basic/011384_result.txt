a tiny scottish hamlet named towie is bracing itself for the world 's next gold rush - after prospectors found evidence that it sits on major deposits . the remote community in aberdeenshire does not even have a shop but local streams have been well known for grains of gold of ' significant size ' for decades . sleepy towie , population 300 , has a single road , a few houses , primary school , church , graveyard and a community hall .
