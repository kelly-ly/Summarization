( cnn ) the university of michigan has decided to proceed with a screening of the film `` american sniper '' despite objections from some students . more than 200 students signed a petition asking the school not to show the movie as part of umix , a series of social events the university stages for students . on wednesday , e. royster harper , university of michigan 's vice president for student life , said in a statement that `` it was a mistake to cancel the showing of the movie ' american sniper ' on campus as part of a social event for students '' and that the show will go on .
