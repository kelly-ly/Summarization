hibernian claimed their first edinburgh derby victory of the season as goals from jason cummings and farid el alagui sealed a 2-0 win over hearts and allowed them to collect a valuable three points in the push for second place in the championship . hearts improved after the break but hibs sealed the win in the first minute of injury time with a cool finish from el alagui . on 12 minutes , fraser fyvie lifted a first-time curling effort over the crossbar from 18 yards before hearts full-back callum paterson headed the ball onto the top of his own bar from a teasing allan free-kick .
