rome ( cnn ) muslims who were among migrants trying to get from libya to italy in a boat this week threw 12 fellow passengers overboard -- killing them -- because the 12 were christians , italian police said thursday . italian authorities have arrested 15 people on suspicion of murdering the christians at sea , police in palermo , sicily , said . sometime during the trip north across the mediterranean sea , the alleged assailants -- muslims from the ivory coast , mali and senegal -- threw the 12 overboard , police said .
