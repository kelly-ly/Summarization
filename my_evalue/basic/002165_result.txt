a senior high school teacher from anshun city has been dubbed the ' luckiest wife in china ' after her soldier husband cooked her enough food to last for a year before he returned to service . husband yin yunfeng spent the last week of his annual holiday making all sorts of stir-fried dishes as well as about 1,000 dumplings as a way to show his love to his wife , according to thepeople 's daily online . the devoted husband is worried that his wife would be eating instant noodles all the time so he cooked different types of dishes and put them into small foam containers to be frozen .
