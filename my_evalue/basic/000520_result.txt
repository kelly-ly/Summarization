( cnn ) michele bachmann is comparing president obama to the co-pilot of the doomed germanwings flight . `` with his iran deal , barack obama is for the 300 million souls of the united states what andreas lubitz was for the 150 souls on the german wings flight - a deranged pilot flying his entire nation into the rocks , '' the minnesota republican and former representative wrote in a facebook comment posted march 31 . andreas lubitz , the co-pilot of germanwings flight 9525 , is accused by authorities of deliberately crashing the plane in the french alps .
