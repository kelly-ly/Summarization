a craig cathcart goal was enough to put watford top of the championship with just two games of the season remaining following a 1-0 win over birmingham . they looked as though they would have to settle for second after matt ritchie put bournemouth ahead against sheffield wednesday and on course for top spot , but chris maguire 's stoppage-time penalty at the goldsands stadium denied the cherries and left watford on top of the pile . the decisive goal at vicarage road came in the 56th minute when substitute ikechi anya crossed from the left and cathcart sent a fine volleyed finish into the top corner past goalkeeper darren randolph .
