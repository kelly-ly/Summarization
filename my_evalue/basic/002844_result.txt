increasing opposition to a £175 million ‘ garden bridge’ championed by boris johnson could scupper the mayor’s plans for a new green space spanning the thames . local campaigners vigorously opposed to the bridge are calling for a parliamentary inquiry into the scheme ahead of a crucial judicial review next month , the observer reported . the director of the waterloo community development group , michael ball , has said the £175 million could fund 30 million new london parks or the equivalent of 30 times the area of open space the bridge would give .
