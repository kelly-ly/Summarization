quinn patrick was on a fishing trip with his dad at snow lake , indiana when the pair caught a bowfin , videoed lying lifeless on a concrete dock . they are demersal freshwater fish , which means they live and feed at the bottom of lakes and seas . and are piscivore , which means they are carnivorous and predominately eat other fish . common names for the fish include mudfish , dogfish , mud pike , griddle , grinnel , cypress trout and choupique .
