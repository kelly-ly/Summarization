in southern germany , hundreds of people braved the snow for a traditional easter monday procession on horseback , while in slovakia women were doused with buckets of water as part of their weekend celebrations . a similar age-old tradition takes place in hungary , where after being sprinkled with water , the women give the men beautifully coloured eggs in return . the easter horseback parade , known as the georgiritt , is held in traustein , germany and dates back to the 18th century .
