proud and unabashed , aston villa supporting talksport commentator stan collymore showed his true colours when he watched his boyhood club claw back a vital point against premier league relegation rivals qpr . sporting a claret and blue scarf in the press box at villa park , the former striker could not contain himself as christian benteke fired home to make it 2-1 to the home side and was pictured leaping out of his seat . collymore himself played for villa between 1997 and 2000 , having grown up idolosing the likes of gary shaw and peter withe . he has been a talksport commentator since 2008 .
