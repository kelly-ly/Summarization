abc 's world news tonight evening newscast has officially overtaken underfire rival nbc 's nightly news in the ratings war for the first time in more than five and a half years . nbc 's 288 consecutive week winning run had stetched back to september 2009 and the drop from the top spot comes two months after anchor brian williams received a six-month suspension for misleading viewers about his coverage of the iraq war in 2003 . world news also won the week among that demographic , 1.95 million viewers compared to nbc 's 1.79 million . many inside nbc news continue to advocate for the well-liked veteran anchor , who has been a stabilizing force at a broadcast and a news division rocked by a series of missteps .
