chinese hospitals are harvesting up to 11,000 organs from political prisoners without anaesthetic every year , according to a new documentary . it reveals red cross estimations that just 37 people are registered organ donors in china , even though the country has the second highest rate of transplants in the world . one former health worker is quoted as saying : ‘ i testify to the atrocious crime that the hospital committed in removing livers and corneas from living falun gong members . ‘ chinese officials have denied the allegations , saying that organs are taken from volunteers , the sydney morning herald reports .
