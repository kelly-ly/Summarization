a war hero pilot who survived almost 70 skirmishes with the luftwaffe has died after being knocked down by a hit-and-run driver near his home . mr gregory began flying during the second world war and was awarded the distinguished flying cross for his gallantry in 69 combat missions over nazi-occupied europe . mr gregory was performing loop-the-loops throughout his 80s – earning the nickname the flying zimmer – as a founder member of the great war display team , a band of pilots determined to preserve the spirit and skills of britain’s first world war dogfighter heroes .
