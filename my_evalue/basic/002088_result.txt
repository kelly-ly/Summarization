a california county on tuesday approved paying a $ 650,000 settlement to avoid a lawsuit by a man whose beating by deputies after a horse chase was captured on video . san bernardino county spokesman david wert said county supervisors approved the settlement with 30-year-old francis pusok in a closed meeting . pusok 's arrest was recorded by a tv news helicopter and has led to an fbi civil rights investigation and 10 deputies being placed on leave pending an internal probe .
