a chinese hospital is being painstakingly moved just eight metres a day on 1,000 metal rollers to prevent it from being demolished to make way for a new road . the large brick building is 1,700 square metres in size and needs more than 1,000 wheels to ' roll ' it . now , the two-storey hospital outpatient building in the city of zhengzhou , in central china , is being slowly and carefully pushed just a few metres a day on the giant cylinder-shaped rollers . it will take three days of careful and slow rolling to move the hospital building 17.5m to its new , safe spot .
