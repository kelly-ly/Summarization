( cnn ) an american citizen was wounded by gunfire thursday as she drove from the medical school in karachi , pakistan , where she works , police said . debra lobo , a 55-year-old california native , was shot in the right cheek and left arm and is unconscious but expected to survive , according to mohamad shah , a karachi police spokesman . police found pamphlets that the assailants had thrown into lobo 's car , written in urdu , saying `` america should be burnt , '' shah said .
