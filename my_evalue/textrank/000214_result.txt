in south korea , myopia rates among 20-year-olds have leaped from 18 % in 1955 to over 96 % myopia in 2011 . in china , students have been mandated by its education ministry to perform daily eye exercises . as people age , they can become at higher risk for severe eye disorders such as high myopia , glaucoma ( optic nerve damage ) , cataracts ( clouding of the lens ) and retinal detachment .
