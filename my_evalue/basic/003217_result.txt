a paralyzed former police officer who advocated for wheelchair users has died after accidentally driving his atv off an idaho cliff and plunging 500 feet . tom mctevia , 42 , who was left without the use of his legs following another atv accident 11 years ago , was traveling with a group and driving his friend tina hoisington , 45 , near an overlook at lake pend oreille in bonner county on sunday . when the group stopped for a photograph around noon , mctevia 's atv got too close to the edge of the 2,000-foot cliff and went over , witnesses told selkirk fire rescue , the cda press reported .
