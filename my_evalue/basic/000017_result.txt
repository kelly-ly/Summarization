nairobi , kenya ( cnn ) university of nairobi students were terrified sunday morning when they heard explosions -- caused by a faulty electrical cable -- and believed it was a terror attack , the school said . students on the kikuyu campus stampeded down the halls of the kimberly dormitory , and some jumped from its fifth floor , the university said . on sunday , as many as 108 students from the university of nairobi were admitted to kenyatta national hospital .
