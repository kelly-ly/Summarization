( cnn ) eleven channels associated with the french-language global television network tv5monde went black late wednesday due to an `` extremely powerful cyberattack , '' the network 's director said . in addition to its 11 channels , tv5monde also temporarily lost control of its social media outlets and its websites , director yves bigot said in a video message posted later on facebook . but there was no immediate claim of responsibility by isis or any other group . according to france 's ministry of culture and communications , tv5monde offers round-the-clock entertainment , news and culturing programming in french that reaches 260 million homes worldwide .
