during the 19th century inuits in greenland would have entertained polite company in their settlements while wearing a thong made of seal fur . traditionally known as a ' naatsit ' , the underwear is adorned with beads and would have been sewn together by a woman using strips of seal pelt . seal fur provides less insulation than caribou fur to prevents the wearer from sweating and causing the material to become damp , and later freeze in the cold . the severe conditions of the region means that clothes have to protect against the cold , wind and damp and seal fur , in particular , provides less insulation than caribou fur .
