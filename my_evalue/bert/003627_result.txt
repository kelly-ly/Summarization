prehistoric humans living in a cave 14,700 years ago were cannibals and made cups from the skulls of the dead , a new study has found . human skulls found in the cave had also been extenisvely shaped to create cups or bowls . simon parfitt , an archaeologist from university college london , said : ' a recurring theme of this period is the remarkable rarity of burials and how commonly we find human remains mixed with occupation waste at many sites . it was a very careful process - they were really trying to create an object .
