st. john , u.s. virgin islands ( cnn ) government officials are trying to track down vacationers who stayed at villas in the virgin islands who may have been exposed to a deadly pesticide . local officials said methyl bromide is suspected to have been used improperly several times in the u.s. virgin islands , in different parts of the island ; even the governor said his condominium complex was fumigated with it in 2013 , without his knowledge . dawn henry , the commissioner designee of the local department of planning and natural resources , or dpnr , said that while investigating what happened , the agency found methyl bromide was likely also used last fall at the same sirenusa resort , as well as in a vacation villa in st. croix and in two nontourist locations .
