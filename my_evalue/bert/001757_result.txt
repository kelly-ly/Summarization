chelsea supporters are being invited to take part in a protest photo aimed at improving the rights and welfare of construction workers building the stadiums for the 2022 world cup in qatar . photo protest will take place outside stamford bridge at 3pm on saturday ahead of manchester united game . immigrant workers are paid pennies by the hour - some going without any pay whatsoever - and are bound to their employer , unable to change jobs or even leave the country without permission , for which there is no obligation to be given .
