three florida prison officers who belong to the ku klux klan have been charged with plotting to kill a black inmate after his release , officials said on thursday . klans men thomas jordan driver , 25 , david elliot moran , 47 , and charles thomas newcomb , 42 , each face one state count of conspiracy to commit murder , according to florida attorney general pam bondi 's office . the state said the murder plot started after driver , an officer at the department of corrections reception and medical center in gainesville , rural north florida , had a fight with the inmate .
