paul scholes has criticised mario mandzukic for over reacting after the atletico madrid striker appeared to be punched and bitten by real madrid 's daniel carvajal . mandzukic was the centre of attention in on-pitch scraps with sergio ramos , raphael varane and carvajal during the champions league quarter-final first leg at the vicente calderon . the croatian striker was on the receiving end of a number of hits from real defenders , even having his face cut following a ramos elbow , but scholes claimed the atletico star was over-reacting to the challenges made on him .
