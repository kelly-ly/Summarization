jessica ennis-hill has revealed she faces her ' biggest challenge ever ' as she attempts to juggle being a mother with winning gold at next year 's rio olympics . britain 's golden girl of athletics is ready to step back on the track nine months after the birth of son reggie and next month she will take on katarina-johnson thompson , 22 , who has risen to prominence in ennis-hill 's absence , winning pentathlon gold at the european indoor championships last month and taking her british record . ennis-hill will compete against katarina johnson-thompson in austria at the end of may .
