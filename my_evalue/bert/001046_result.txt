( cnn ) lest we americans forget that -- in the wellsprings of our nation -- france bore the torch of liberty alongside us , our old ally has launched a reminder from across the atlantic 's waves . l'hermione , with three sail masts and bright royal blue and gold markings , is a painstaking replica of an 18th century french frigate that fought with the united states ' founding fathers in the war of independence . in chesapeake bay , it joined the blockade that led the british to surrender . l'hermione will ply across the atlantic for 27 days en route to yorktown , virginia , where it plans to arrive in early june .
