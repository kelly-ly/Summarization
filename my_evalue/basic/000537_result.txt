( cnn ) a new york jury deliberating the fate of the man charged with the 1979 killing of 6-year-old etan patz is struggling to reach a verdict . deliberations resumed on thursday , one day after new york supreme court justice maxwell wiley ordered the jury to continue efforts to reach a decision on the guilt or innocence of a bodega worker named pedro hernandez . etan patz 's parents have waited more than 35 years for justice , but some have questioned whether that is even possible in hernandez 's case .
