telecom watchdogs are to stop a rip-off that allows mobile phone firms to charge customers to call supposedly free 0800 numbers . ernest doku , telecoms expert at the price comparison service , uswitch.com , said the end to mobile charges to call 0800 numbers was a ‘long-overdue victory for common-sense’ . at the moment , the cost of calling these numbers is mired in confusion with charges varying enormously depending on whether you are using a landline or a mobile phone . ofcom and phone companies will be publicising the changes under the banner uk calling before the switch in the summer .
