a lesbian couple are suing a georgia sperm bank for false advertising - claiming their ' phd student , eloquent-speaking ' donor turned out to be a schizophrenic with a criminal record . angela collins and margaret elizabeth hanson of port hope , ontario , decided to use xytex corp to start a family in 2006 , believing they had a strict vetting process . in june 2014 , the pair discovered their donor was called james christian aggeles , according to the lawsuit . they also claim they have suffered emotional and financial hardship as a result .
