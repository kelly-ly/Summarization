the horseshoe-shaped glass walkway in chongqing extends 87.5ft from the edge of a cliff , allowing visitors to feel as though they are walking on air with the valley floor 2,350ft below their feet . with jaw-dropping panoramic views from its observation deck , it is the longest cantilever bridge in the world , beating arizona’s grand canyon skywalk by 16.4ft . the bridge can accommodate up to 200 visitors at once , who each pay an admission fee of 60 yuan ( approximately £6.30 or $ 9.60 ) officials said the bridge was designed to withstand an 8.0-magnitude earthquake and level 14 typhoon , china radio international reported .
