the humongous attraction on staten island promises stunning panoramic views of the manhattan skyline , in addition to other new york city boroughs and the neighbouring state of new jersey . located next to the hudson river the 630ft new york wheel will be able to carry as many as 1,400 passengers at a time , with one full cycle taking about 38 minutes to complete . ticket prices will range from an estimated $ 25 to $ 30 ( £17 to £20 ) as new york wheel llc , the company behind the ambitious project , hopes it will join the statue of liberty , empire state building and times square as one of new york’s must-visit attractions .
