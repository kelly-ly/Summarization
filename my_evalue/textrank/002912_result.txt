manny pacquiao revealed yet another of his talents by releasing his own walk-out tune ahead of his $ 300m mega-fight with floyd mayweather jnr . the tune , which sounds somewhat like an 80s rock ballad actually translates as ' i will fight for the fillipino ' and will be played ahead of the richest fight in history against the undefeated american at the mgm grand on may 2 . multi-talented pacquiao not only wrote and sang the tune but also directed the music video . meanwhile , the 36-year-old decided to train on the streets of los angeles on friday as he ran along the trail at griffith park joined by several fitness fanatics . as well as working on his aerobic endurance , pacquiao was also tested on his core strength and showed off his impressive footwork in front of watching spectators by shadow boxing .
