a 1921 application form to join the racist ku klux klan organisation has emerged , which asks ‘ are you of the white race or of a colored race ? ’ the form is ominously titled ‘ invisible empire’ , an alternative name for the clan at the time , born from founder nathan bedford forrest’s claim that the group was ‘invisible’ . the kkk was founded in 1866 in tennessee , with forrest becoming the first leader , or grand wizard , in 1867 . today it 's thought to number in the low thousands , with most people affiliated with it living in america 's deep south .
