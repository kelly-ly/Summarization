manchester city are preparing bids for english trio raheem sterling , ross barkley and jay rodriguez as part of a summer anglification of their squad . the club are well aware of a lack of homegrown talent at senior level with the departure of frank lampard and uncertainty over james milner 's future exacerbating the problem . etihad stadium chiefs have moved to address the situation and are expected to lodge offers for liverpool winger sterling , everton midfielder barkley and southampton striker rodriguez .
