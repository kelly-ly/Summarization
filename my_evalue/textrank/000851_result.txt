a small boat carrying about 50 migrants left from the area of le borgne , west of cap-haitien , on wednesday night . it began to sail toward the island of providenciales in the turks and caicos when it was caught in bad weather , civil protection spokesman joseph edgar celestin said . rescuers , most of them volunteers from the town of le borgne and surrounding communities , were dispatched to the scene and rescued at least 12 people , celestin said .
