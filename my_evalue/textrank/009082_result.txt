anti-semitic attacks surged worldwide by 38 per cent last year , with the highest number of incidents occurring in france , an annual study has revealed . anger in western europe over israel 's military offensive in the gaza strip last july and august , as well as anti-jewish caricatures in the media and social networks had helped to turn anti-semitism into an ' almost daily phenomena seen and felt in every sphere of life ' , the report said . in france , the number of anti-semitic attacks rose to 164 in 2014 compared with 141 in 2013 .
