the family of conjoined twin sisters who died 19 days after they were born have been left mortified after they arrived at their gravesite to find cemetery staff had cleared the baby section of all mementos and tossed them in the rubbish . their family and friends had built up a small memorial with pink and white statues , flowers , pebbles and toys over the past 11 months . family members have visited the grave every week to leave mementos and flowers for faith and hope , but when parents simon howie and renee young arrived on thursday they found the site completely bare . the couple retrieved what they could find and returned later in the afternoon to see what else they could salvage - by then everything had been laid out on shelves for people to collect .
