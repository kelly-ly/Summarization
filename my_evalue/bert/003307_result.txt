at first sight it might appear shocking that a clapped-out car such as this would be on the market for almost a quarter of a million pounds . despite its appearance it remains an attractive prospect for many car enthusiasts by offering the rare chance to restore a virtually-untouched db4 . the car - to be auctioned in buckinghamshire - was off the road in storage when bought by the unnamed current owner in 1982 as a restoration project . the car , registered as btb 478a , is being sold at bonhams ' aston martin sale in newport pagnell , buckinghamshire , on may 9 .
