tripoli , libya ( cnn ) it took one somali woman seven months and 4,000 miles to trek to libya . they , like male migrants , are willing to risk their lives on crowded boats to make the final part of the trip . libyan officials are in a quandary . gemma parkin , a spokeswoman for save the children , said that the number of migrants who have fled to find refuge in europe has skyrocketed 70 % this year over last , a dramatic rise that she attributed mostly to the deteriorating security situation in libya .
