a soviet dissident who spent 12 years in prison and forced labour camps is to be charged with making and possessing indecent images of children after a police investigation . vladimir bukovsky , 72 , has lived in the uk since he fled the soviet union in the 1970s after he was accused of spreading anti-soviet propaganda . bukovsky is a dissident , author and activist who spent 12 years in soviet prisons , forced-labour camps and forced-treatment psychiatric hospitals . bukovsky will appear at cambridge magistrates court , pictured , on may 5 after receiving a summons .
