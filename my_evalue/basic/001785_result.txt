 tens of millions of people in china marked the annual ' tomb sweeping festival ' this weekend by travelling home to their ancestral villages . around 1.3 billion chinese people marked the qingming festival , a three-day holiday in china , by watching parades and travelling home . in shaanxi province , a huge outdoor celebration was held as part of the qingming festival and in honour of the ' yellow emperor ' huang di , considered the founding father of the chinese nation .
