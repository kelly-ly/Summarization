( cnn ) at least 54 people have died and 15 others are missing after a russian fishing vessel sank off the kamchatka peninsula , according to russia 's state-run tass news agency . the dalniy vostok freezer trawler -- a commercial fishing vessel -- was carrying 132 people , the ministry said . more than 20 fishing vessels are searching for the 15 people still thought to be missing , tass said .
