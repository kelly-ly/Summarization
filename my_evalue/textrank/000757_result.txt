as a result of the recent spikes in hiv and hepatitis c infections among injecting drug users in rural indiana and kentucky , the controversial topic of syringe exchange programs has come to the fore again . for states such as new york and washington -- early adopters of these interventions -- syringe services programs have played a crucial role in driving down hiv transmission among injecting drug users . for example , an analysis by johns hopkins university researchers showed that expanding the availability of syringe services programs to cover just 10 % of all injections in the united states would prevent almost 500 new hiv infections among drug users per year .
