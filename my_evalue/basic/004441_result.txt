middlesbrough 's patrick bamford has been named the championship player of the year at the football league awards . bamford , on loan at the riverside stadium from chelsea , received the award at the gala ceremony in london on sunday evening in front of over 600 guests , following a vote among club managers . the 21-year-old beat competition from ipswich town 's daryl murphy and watford 's troy deeney to claim the overall sky bet championship player of the year award .
