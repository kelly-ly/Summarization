melamchi , nepal ( cnn ) even from high above , flying in an indian air force helicopter , it is easy to see that the people of melamchi , central nepal , are happy to see us . residents in this remote village , about a 44km drive from nepal 's capital , kathmandu , stand on the distinctive steeply terraced hillsides and wave furiously as the relief flight passes overhead . the mission , a joint effort between indian air crew and a nepalese army medical team , is only the third operation of its kind to reach the village since saturday 's massive 7.8-magnitude quake , which left more than 5,000 people dead .
