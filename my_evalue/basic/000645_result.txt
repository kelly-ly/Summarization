sao paulo , brazil ( cnn ) throngs of protesters packed the streets of major brazilian cities on sunday , pushing for the impeachment of president dilma rousseff . fueled by mounting anger over a corruption scandal that has implicated politicians in rousseff 's party , demonstrators chanted `` out with dilma '' and `` time for change . '' a sea of protesters dressed in the green and yellow of the brazilian flag used decades-old rallying cries to fire up their ranks , singing rock songs that date back to protests of the country 's one-time military dictatorship .
