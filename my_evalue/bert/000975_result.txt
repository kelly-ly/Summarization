( cnn ) one israeli citizen was killed and another injured in what police are calling a suspected terror attack wednesday night near hebrew university in jerusalem . israel police spokesman micky rosenfeld said a 37-year-old arab motorist from east jerusalem struck two people standing at a bus stop in the french hill section of the city . a 20-year-old woman remains in serious condition , according to rosenfeld . the driver has been arrested and is under investigation by the general security services .
