( cnn ) the presence of a harmful pesticide at a luxury villa in the u.s. virgin islands may have resulted in the illness of a delaware family , the u.s. environmental protection agency said friday . paramedics were called last week to a rented villa at the sirenusa resort in st. john after the family of four fell ill . `` our preliminary results do show that there was a presence of methyl bromide in the unit where the family was staying , '' said elias rodriguez , an epa spokesman .
