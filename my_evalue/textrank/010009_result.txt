white solidarity groups in south africa are protesting over the removal of a statue of british colonialist cecil rhodes from the university of cape town . the government has backed the move , and sandile memela , the spokesman for the arts and culture ministry , said they are working to see if the country can take an ‘ official position’ on statues and other colonial symbols . while his supporters say he brought political and physical infrastructure to south africa , others say his actions laid the groundwork for apartheid by restricting the rights of black africans to work .
