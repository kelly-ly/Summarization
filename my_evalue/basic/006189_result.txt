rory mcilroy will tee off his bid to become just the sixth man to win golf 's grand slam alongside phil mickelson and ryan moore at augusta national on thursday . the northern irishman will be joined over the first two rounds by three-time masters champion mickelson and another mercurial american in moore in what promises to be an electric three-ball when they tee off at 3.41pm bst . but all eyes will be trained on tiger woods among the later starters , as the four-time green jacket winner returns to action for the first time since february alongside two stars of last year 's ryder cup at gleneagles , jamie donaldson and jimmy walker .
