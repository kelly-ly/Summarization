children of the 1970s and 1980s will likely remember the chunky , plastic fisher-price magnets that adorned fridges in family homes . this graphic shows the results for 400 synesthetes with 10 or more letters matching the toy . among those born in the decade after the toy began to be manufactured , the proportion of synesthetes with learned letter-colour pairings was closer to 15 per cent . there have also been studies in which researchers claimed to have taught people to be synaesthetic - and as a result it is a widely contested condition .
