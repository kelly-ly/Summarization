manchester united have ramped up their efforts to sign psv eindhoven star memphis depay by making an official approach for the 21-year-old . the eredivisie champions have confirmed that the red devils have made a move over the holland international - with united manager louis van gaal keen to make him one of his first signings this summer . van gaal is already familiar with the qualities of depay having named him in his 23-man world cup squad last year as holland finished third in brazil .
