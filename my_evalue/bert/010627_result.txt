timothy mcveigh considered the bombing of the oklahoma city federal building 20 years ago a failure because the structure was still standing after the blast that killed 168 people . mcveigh also viewed himself as a ' paul revere-type messenger ' and even suggested his defense team should receive $ 800,000 from the government , according an archive of documents donated by the convicted bomber 's lead attorney . a photo taken on april 27 , 1995 , shows damage to the alfred p murrah federal building after the truck bomb . the archive includes a confidential report from a polygraph examiner , who wrote that mcveigh had wanted to `take out ' the murrah building on april 19 , 1995 .
