chinese designers have developed an contraption called the bike washing machine that contains a washing machine drum in its front wheel . they say , however , that the device could be a solution for those who want both a washing machine and an exercise bike but can not find space for both . when you ride this bike , the pedalling motion causes the drum of the washing machine to rotate , at the same time , the superfluous electricity is generated which can be used to power the display screen or stored for future use .
