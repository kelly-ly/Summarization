an australian couple who abandoned a boy born in a surrogacy deal in india were repeatedly told the child would be left stateless , new evidence shows . australian government officials had full knowledge of the startling ordeal , according to documents obtained the abc . conversations between the australian high commission in new delhi and government officials in canberra detail how the couple travelled to india in late 2012 and seeked citizenship for a baby girl but told consular staff they would be leaving her twin brother behind because they could not afford him . there were also fears last year that the boy may have been sold .
