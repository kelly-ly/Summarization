( cnn ) parts of miami-dade county 's skyline was hidden from view monday as smoke from a growing 1,850-acre wildfire loomed over portions of the florida county . what started as a nonthreatening and seemingly shrinking grass fire on sunday , consuming fewer than 100 acres according to miami-dade fire rescue battalion chief al cruz , grew to be more than 10 times that within the next 24 hours . by monday night , the fire had burned nearly 2,000 acres and was 50 % contained , the fire department said .
