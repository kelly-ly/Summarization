mk dons boss karl robinson hailed the patience of his team as they beat doncaster 3-0 to keep the heat on preston at the top of league one . robinson’s team needed a freakish goal by dean bowditch , 25 minutes from time , to break doncaster’s resistance and stay within a point of the automatic promotion places with two games to play . ‘it’s our first time this season we’ve had a fluke goal , it’s overdue , ’ said robinson after carl baker , another substitute , had secured the win with two fine goals .
