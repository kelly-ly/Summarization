incredible footage of a sydney train station flooding during the storm of a decade on wednesday has been captured on cctv . a time lapse video , filmed from the platform over a 45 minute period at 11am , shows the water creeping along the tracks at bardwell park in sydney 's south forcing the partial closure of the t2 airport line . at one point a worker in a high-vis jacket appears to look at the rising water to take a photo . an evacuation order remains in place for parts of milperra in sydney , about 200,000 remain without power and the ses is still working to address 50 per cent of 12,420 calls for help it has received since monday morning .
