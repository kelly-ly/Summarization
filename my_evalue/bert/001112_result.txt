new jersey police have released a sketch of an infant whose headless remains were discovered last year in an attempt to track down the child 's mother . on nov. 11 , 2014 , the headless body of a seven-pound-five-ounce infant was found in a trash heap in farmingdale , new jersey . the investigation is ongoing and monmouth county crime stoppers is offering up to $ 5,000 for information leading to the identity of the infant 's mother .
