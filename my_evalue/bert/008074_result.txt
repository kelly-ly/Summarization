an american who suffered third-degree burns to 70 per cent of his body in a childhood accident has faced his fears by becoming a firefighter . brave : mr mccarty lost part of his fingers and thumbs in the accident and had to teach himself how to quickly put on his uniform and air tank and properly brace himself with his hip when using a hose . he said : ' the one thing that got me through was the love of my family - especially my brothers , ryan and jason . ' he and his brothers became inseparable and with their encouragement and support he found the courage to stop hiding himself , and accept the stares and comments .
