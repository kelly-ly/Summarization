it has long been considered the most expensive place in the uk to buy property , but it seems london is being pipped to the post by coastal salcombe . in the pricey west london neighbourhood of notting hill , property prices average £1,629,465 . nearby seaside competitors , like sandbanks , aldeburgh and padstow , however , have seen a dip in prices of about eight per cent on average . in fact , the most recent assessment places a view of the salcombe estuary as being worth £1.3 million , given the average property cost of over £600,000 and an average waterfront property price of £2million .
