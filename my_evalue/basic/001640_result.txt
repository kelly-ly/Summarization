an air force veteran and former playboy model was arrested for taking an american flag from campus protesters who were trampling on it . michelle manhart , 38 , was handcuffed by police at valdosta state university , georgia , and driven off in a patrol car after grabbing the stars and stripes and refusing to return it to the student demonstrators . video footage of the event , on friday , shows manhart struggling with officers , who force her to the ground after she refuses to let the flag go .
