a hit-and-run driver who killed a pregnant 21-year-old woman , her husband and their unborn child has been sentenced to life in prison . the driver , who was convicted in february , is thought to have been traveling in his bmw at 70mph , twice the speed limit , when the incident occurred just after midnight on march 3 , 2013 . nachman and raizel glaubers , who were 7 months pregnant at the time of the crash , were heading to the hospital in this toyota camry livery cab when they were killed . he was eventually taken into custody four days after the accident , when he surrendered himself to police after arranging to meet them in bethlehem , pennsylvania .
