( cnn ) nine british citizens were arrested in turkey on wednesday , suspected of trying to cross illegally into syria , the turkish military said on its website . it did n't say why the group allegedly was trying to get into syria , which has been torn by a roughly four-year war between syrian government forces and islamist extremist groups and other rebels . the british foreign office said wednesday that it is aware of reports of the arrests and that it is seeking information about the incident from turkish authorities .
