a pair of british expats say they are ‘trapped’ in southern spain amid an ongoing legal battle with the local town hall over a luxury bed and breakfast they once operated . patrick and valerie jubb are attempting to sell the four-bedroom property where they currently reside in jimena de la frontera , but it is now on the brink of being torn down . the retired couple told spain’s olive press news website that they have done nothing wrong but face losing the estate over illegal additions made by a previous owner .
