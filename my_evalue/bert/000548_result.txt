( cnn ) as his military career winds down , britain 's prince harry is going to be spending some time down under . harry `` will work and live alongside colleagues in the australian army in a number of regiments in sydney , darwin and perth , '' the australian defence forces said thursday . harry will also spend time meeting wounded , injured and ill service members in australia . before he reports for duty monday , the prince will lay a wreath at the war memorial in the australian capital , canberra .
