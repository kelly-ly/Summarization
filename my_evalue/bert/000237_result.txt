tokyo ( cnn ) the operator of the crippled fukushima daiichi nuclear plant has given up trying to recover a robotic probe after it stopped moving inside one of the reactors . tokyo electric power company ( tepco ) deployed the remote-controlled robot on friday inside one of the damaged reactors that had suffered a meltdown following a devastating earthquake and tsunami in 2011 . it managed to collect data on radiation levels in 14 of the 18 targeted locations . cnn 's yoko wakatsuki reported from tokyo , japan and naomi ng wrote from hong kong .
