washington believes that the ‘ special relationship’ between britain and the us is over , according to a secret briefing document seen by the mail on sunday . the document – prepared by the congressional research service , an in-house intelligence body that gives confidential analysis to legislators – states that while britain and the us are likely to ‘ remain key economic partners’ , a ‘ reassessment of the special relationship may be in order… because its geopolitical setting has been changing’ . prime minister david cameron and us president barack obama serve food during a downing street barbecue in may 2011 . increasingly , however , the relationship has come to be seen as one-sided , with british prime ministers more keen to flag up the alliance than us presidents .
