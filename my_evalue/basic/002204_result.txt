a replica of the warship that carried france 's marquis de lafayette to help american colonists in their war of independence sets sail for the united states on saturday , symbolic of a historic moment that binds the two nations . lafayette crossed the atlantic on the original hermione in 1780 to tell his friend george washington , commander of the american insurgents against british imperial rule , that france was sending a strong military force to help them . the replica of an 18th century french navy frigate l'hermione , set sail on its maiden voyage to the united states off the coast of fouras , southwestern france on saturday .
