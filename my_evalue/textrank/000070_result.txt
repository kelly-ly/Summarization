( cnn ) lois lilienstein , co-star of `` sharon , lois & bram 's elephant show '' — the canadian preschool show that ran on nickelodeon during the early 1990s — has died , aged 78 . the tv show `` sharon , lois & bram 's elephant show '' ran on cbc in canada from 1984 to 1989 , before continuing in reruns on nickelodeon stateside until 1995 . sally catto , general manager , programming at cbc television , paid tribute to lilienstein for her contribution to canadian kids tv .
