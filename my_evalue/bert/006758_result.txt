flying for business or pleasure is one of the most thrilling experiences in the world , but it remains full of mystery for the average traveller . starting a plane is ‘ a little more complicated’ than starting a car , said captain piers applegarth , a representative of the british airline pilots association ( balpa ) . like passengers , the flight crew can bring their own food on board , but the meals provided to them are the same or variations of the meals provided to passengers in business class . the boeing 747 , the original jumbo jet , has a lavatory just for pilots .
