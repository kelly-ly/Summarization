18-year-old xie xu met his 19-year-old classmate zhang chi when the pair began attending school together at daxu high school in xuzhou , a large city in the northern province of jiangsu , in china . zhang suffers from muscular dystrophy , a condition that weakens skeletal muscle which often makes it very difficult for those with the disease to walk or carry their own weight . the teenagers live in dorms close by to the school , and each day xie carries zhang from their rooms to the school , and back again , and helps him with daily tasks such as getting meals and washing clothes .
