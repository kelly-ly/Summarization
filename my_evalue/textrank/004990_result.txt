floyd mayweather has taken a very public swipe at his ' haters ' ahead of his fight-of-the-century bout with manny pacquiao next month . with the two fighters ' management teams and the hosts mgm grand locked in a stand-off between ticket allocations , they have yet to go on public sale . millions of dollars are at stake in the dispute because after the mgm takes its share , each camp gets a certain percentage of tickets and is able to resell them with ticket brokers for higher prices .
