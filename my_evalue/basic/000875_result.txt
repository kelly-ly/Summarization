( cnn ) five young chinese feminists , whose detention has provoked an international outcry , may face up to five years in prison over their campaign for gender equality . the women were among detained on march 6 and march 7 in three chinese cities -- beijing , guangzhou and hangzhou -- shortly before events they had planned for international women 's day on march 8 . wang qiushi , the lawyer for one of the women , wei tingting , said police had recommended on april 6 that prosecutors press charges of `` assembling a crowd to disturb public order .
