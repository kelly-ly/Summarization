nairobi , kenya ( cnn ) kenyan deputy president william ruto on saturday gave the united nations ' refugee agency three months to relocate refugees from the dadaab camp -- the world 's largest -- to somalia , or `` we shall relocate them ourselves . '' the agency and the governments of both kenya and somalia agreed in 2013 on a process for the repatriation of somali refugees . it 's not clear where he expects the refugees to go , other than somewhere into somalia and out of kenya .
