nearly 90 years after she would have graduated from high school , a 103-year-old wisconsin woman finally got her diploma . now a resident at meadows assisted living and memory care in spring green , the centenarian was presented with an honorary degree by river valley high school , 87 years after her fellow classmates graduated . i was born in 1911 , and there was no way that country kids six miles away could go to high school , ' she said , adding that instead she stayed at home and helped to take care of her eight younger siblings .
