( cnn ) the number of new hiv infections in a rural indiana county has grown , according to the centers for disease control and prevention . the institute is working with state health leaders to control the `` severe outbreak , '' which has spread among users of a prescription opioid called opana . the cdc and state health leaders held a news conference friday to talk about the new numbers and about the growing threat of the spread of disease from iv drug use , especially in isolated rural areas that have sparse health resources .
