antiquities looted from ancient temples and buddhist sites in india have been found on display at the honolulu museum of art after a tourist spotted they were sourced from an art dealer facing charges in india . the museum yesterday handed over seven rare artifacts in its possession after authorities found , to the shock of museum officials , that they had been smuggled into the u.s. by former new york art dealer subhash kapoor . the discovery was made after a visitor last year recognized the name of kapoor 's new york gallery as the source of a 2,000-year-old terra cotta rattle and contacted authorities , said stephan jost , the museum 's director .
