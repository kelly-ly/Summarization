kfc has come under fire for hiring a food stylist in a bid to change its image . the third programme in the series focused on the methods employed by the creative team behind kfc 's rebranding exercise and showed scenes of a food stylist using hairdryers and tweezers when preparing the food for an advertisement . in the final episode , which aired yesterday , kfc is shown about to launch their new pulled chicken range which consists of a twister , a mini wrap and the ultimate burger ( pulled chicken piled on top of fried chicken pieces ) .
