during world war i , dnp was sometimes used to fill armour-piercing shells , as a mixture with picric acid , known as shellite or tridite . the commercial use of dnp is as a pesticide or herbicide and , in 2009 , 11 workers in a chinese chemical factory – plus nine of their relatives – contracted dnp poisoning , their skins turned yellow or even black and two died . dnp was banned in the united states under the food , drug , and cosmetic act of 1938 ; the us food and drug administration said that dnp was too toxic to be used under any circumstance .
