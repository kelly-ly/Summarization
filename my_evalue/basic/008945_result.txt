klopp is leaving borussia dortmund after seven seasons in which he has won two bundesliga titles , the german cup and reached the champions league final , in 2013 at wembley . manager jurgen klopp announced he would be leaving borussia dortmund at the end of the season . borussia dortmund ceo hans-joachim watzke , klopp and michael zorc of dortmund were present for the press conference at signal iduna park on wednesday .
