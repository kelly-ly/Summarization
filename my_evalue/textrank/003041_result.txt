mutombo , a shot-blocking congolese centre whose legendary finger-wagging motion became one of the most recognized gestures in the game , was a four-times nba defensive player of the year and led the league in blocked shots for five straight seasons . mutombo played in the nba from 1991 until 2009 , recording 11,729 points , 12,359 rebounds , and 3,289 blocks during 18 seasons spent mostly with denver , atlanta and houston . three-times college coach of the year john calipari , longtime nba referee dick bavetta , four-times nba all-star spencer haywood , seven-times nba all-star jo jo white and three-times wnba most valuable player lisa leslie were also among the 11 members named to the class of 2015 .
