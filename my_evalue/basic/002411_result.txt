isis has launched english-language radio news bulletins on its iraqi broadcast service - complete with information on the latest suicide bombings and ' martyrdom operations ' . the extremist group 's first english bulletin aired on tuesday on its al-bayan radio network , which already boasts updates in both arabic and russian . the nine-and-a-half minute broadcast , which begins and ends with traditional sounding arabic music , is hosted by a man with an american accent , who takes the listener through the main events of the day .
