the city of eugene in the united states has been awarded the rights to host the 2021 iaaf world championships . the decision was taken by the international association of athletics federations council , who decided to bypass the usual bidding process because of what it saw as a ' unique strategic opportunity to host the iaaf world championships for the first time in the usa ' . qatar captial doha , home to the aspire dome , beat eugene to host the 2019 event . ' in granting the championships to eugene the iaaf council have made a clear choice on a strategic decision that enables us to take advantage of a unique opportunity that may never arise again , whereby public authorities , the private sector , the national olympic committee , nbc and a particularly enthusiastic public are joining forces , ' iaaf president lamine diack said in a statement .
