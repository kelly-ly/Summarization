we all joke about how bad school dinners are but a secondary school in china takes this age old gripe to another level . in protest at their ' horrible ' school meals , high school students in north east china 's jilin province are being forced to the sidewalk everyday to eat their lunch . left , a group of students eat their lunch while sitting on the sidewalk . the school has banned students from bringing food onto the premises and has even erected barbed wire and metal sheeting to prevent smuggling at its back entrance .
