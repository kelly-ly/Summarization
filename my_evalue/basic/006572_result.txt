paris in the spring is the time for lounging on blankets in a park while taking in the spectacular views , sipping on local wine and nibbling on a fresh baguette and ripe camembert . visitors or locals can select made-to-order picnic baskets filled with wine , baguettes , cheese and crisps . le classique picnic , which costs €55 ( £40 ) for two people , comes with a choice of wine , baguettes , an assortment of cheeses , charcuterie , gourmet crisps , fresh fruit , a green salad , water and a seasonal dessert .
