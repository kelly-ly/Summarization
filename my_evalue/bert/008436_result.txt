brad pitt and angelina jolie 's wine from their french vineyard has gone on sale in a british supermarket for the first time after winning rave reviews from critics . the £18-a-bottle wine is produced at the couple 's chateau miraval estate , where they married in august last year after having purchased it for £41million in 2012 . the wine has sold previously in the uk through specialist wine merchants but m & s is the first to offer it up on the high street . the 2014 miraval rose is available in selected m & s stores now .
