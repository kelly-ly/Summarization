( cnn ) larry johnson remembers the fear and feeling of helplessness from being on the skywest airlines flight that made an emergency landing in buffalo , new york . `` i thought we were done , '' he said thursday , one day later . minutes later , johnson says , the attendant announced there was a pressurization problem and told passengers to prepare for the emergency landing . an official with the national transportation safety board told cnn that the agency is in communication with the faa and skywest to gather information on the incident to better understand what took place .
