intelligence quotient , or iq , is a a measure of someone’s intelligence - usually measuring in problem-solving tests - compared to others of their age . mensa says it is ‘ a society for like-minded people’ with the following aims : - to identify and foster human intelligence for the benefit of humanity . - to encourage research in the nature , characteristics , and uses of intelligence . - to provide a stimulating intellectual and social environment for its members . iq is a measure of intelligence , usually referring to a person’s mental agility , and it should not be confused with knowledge , wisdom or memory .
