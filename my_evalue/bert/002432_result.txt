a group of disabled manchester city supporters say they are ' disgusted ' with the club after they hiked prices of some season tickets by £345 to £975 . they claim the premier league champions are trying to force migration to other parts of the etihad stadium as part of a wide-ranging re-seating plan following a 6,000-seat extension which will see capacity rise to around 55,000 . the disabled bays subject to the huge increase are in the same section of the stadium as the season tickets that have risen from £870 to £1,700 - the prime pitchside second tier . the reigning premier league champions celebrate victory over west ham in their last game .
