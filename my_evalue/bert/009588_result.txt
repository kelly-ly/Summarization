a man has died after falling from an apartment balcony while he was on the phone to his new wife . 29-year-old pankaj saw fell three storeys from a macquarie park balcony to his death on thursday , while talking to his wife in india . mr saw was in australia on a long-stay work visa , and had been working on and off in sydney in recent years . speaking to the daily telegraph , howard ryan , director of housesafe , said he believed that the collapse was an accident waiting to happen after taking a look at pictures of the bracket which attaches the balcony to the wall .
