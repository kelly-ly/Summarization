a new study has shown women still have an increased risk of breast cancer years after stopping taking hormone replacement therapy . the women 's health initiative in the us and the million women study in the uk have both revealed strong evidence that hrt increases a woman 's risk of breast , womb and ovarian cancers . now scientists involved in the women 's health initiative study have warned , after further follow-ups , that women who take the combined oestrogen and progesterone hrt therapy continue to have a high risk of breast cancer , up to eight years after they stop treatment .
