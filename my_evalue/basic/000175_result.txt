nairobi ( cnn ) an elite police commando unit waited hours for transport from nairobi , kenya , to garissa , where al-shabaab terrorists had taken over a university on april 2 , according to one of the commandos who participated in the response operation . at 7:00 a.m. local time , the response team was told to assemble for a mission to garissa , the police commando told cnn . once told aircraft would be available , it took the commando team nearly two hours to travel the roughly 22 miles ( 35 kilometers ) to the airport because of the heavy traffic , the commando source said .
