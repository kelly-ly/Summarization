ronny deila insists celtic will not risk their champions league aspirations by embarking on another gruelling pre-season travel schedule . the norwegian blamed last summer’s money-spinning programme of games in the united states , austria , germany for a damaging 4-1 qualifying defeat to legia warsaw . with £16million on offer just for returning to the champions league group stages , however , deila says he has the blessing of chief executive peter lawwell to scale down this summer’s commitments .
