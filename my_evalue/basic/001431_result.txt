a year since the death of former barcelona coach tito vilanova , his memory lives on at the club where he played an integral part in the most successful era in its history . vilanova died aged 45 on april 25 last year after a long battle against parotid gland cancer that cut short the life of a coach in his prime . vilanova was pep guardiola 's assistant during a golden spell for the club when they won three la liga titles and two champions league trophies from 2008-12 .
