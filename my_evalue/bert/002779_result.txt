health officials say a drug-resistant mutant super-strain of a nasty stomach bug has made its way into the u.s. outbreaks of the shigella bacteria are not unusual , but this strain is resistant to the antibiotic most commonly prescribed for adults . cases of shigellosis ( bacteria pictured ) , otherwise known as ' delhi belly ' or ' montezuma 's revenge ' were traced to people who had recently traveled to the dominican republic , india or other countries . sometimes antibiotics are prescribed , especially for more serious cases .
