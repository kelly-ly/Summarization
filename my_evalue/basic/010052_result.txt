sportsmail exclusively revealed last year that bt had agreed to pay £20m over four years for the naming rights of murrayfield — and that the telecommunications giant would follow that up with a deal to have their trademarks on the famous dark blue jersey . the sru insisted the combination of the initial bt investment coupled with the new cash injection from the strip deal had already started to transform scottish rugby . ‘ i am delighted bt will be the new strip partner of the scotland teams and although i can not confirm how much the deal is worth as it is commercially sensitive , i can confirm it is the largest ever shirt sponsorship deal we have done , ’ said dominic mckay , the sru’s director of commercial operations , communications and public affairs .
