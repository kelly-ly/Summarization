jonathan brownlee backed up his win in auckland a fortnight ago by taking the gold coast triathlon in the itu world series on saturday . the yorkshireman moved amongst the leaders on the swim , remained at the front during the cycle and then broke clear in the run , seeing off mario mola by 19 seconds with a winning time of 1:46:53 .
