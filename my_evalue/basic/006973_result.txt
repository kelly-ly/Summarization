a café in eastern china has started to charge customers an 'air-purifying fee ' on top of normal bills after it installed machines to provide fresher air . by charging a one yuan ( 11p ) 'air-purifying fee ' per person , the café hopes customers will pay more attention to the growing pollution issue facing china , reported the people 's daily online . fantasy café is one of the first establishments in china to start charging an 'air-purifying fee ' .
