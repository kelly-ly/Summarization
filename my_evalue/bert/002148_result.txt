the leader of kazakhstan has apologised after winning the presidential election with 97 per cent of the vote saying it would have been ' undemocratic ' for him to make his victory more modest . nazarbayev told a news conference after his victory that if he had interfered in the vote , it would have been undemocratic . nazarbayev has promoted ' a multi-vector policy ' , building good ties with neighbouring russia and china as well as the united states and the european union . but the economy has been hurt in the past year by the decline in global energy prices and by economic difficulty in neighbouring russia , hit by western sanctions over ukraine .
