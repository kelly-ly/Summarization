britain 's obsession with home ownership could be hindering the nation’s economic recovery , a leading global watchdog has said . debt levels are far lower in much of europe , at 72 per cent in spain , around 55 per cent in germany and france , and just over 40 per cent in italy . the survey of 2,000 people found that , of the two-thirds of british adults already in debt from credit cards , overdrafts and loans , a quarter will have to borrow even more to cope . rising estate agents’ fees , removal costs and stamp duty helped push the total bill up 60 per cent between 2004 and last year – double the inflation rate .
