cristiano ronaldo was back to his brilliant , unplayable best as he scored five goals in real madrid 's 9-1 rout of granada on sunday , among them an eight-minute hat-trick . against granada , ronaldo has now scored 11 times in just eight matches . three of those la liga hat-tricks have come against the team from andalusia - he scored triples in a 6-2 win at the ramon sanchez pizjuan in may 2011 , a 4-1 success at the bernabeu in february 2013 and a 7-3 thrashing in october of the same year .
