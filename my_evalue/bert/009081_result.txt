an art collector today claimed she was robbed of more than £4million worth of jewellery during a designer handbag snatch in a notorious motorway tunnel in paris . the ‘ extremely rich’ german , who is part taiwanese , was on her way to loan some of the jewels to the national museum of modern art in paris , according to sources close to the case . the german woman , who has not been named , was in a taxi which was stuck in traffic shortly after arriving at charles de gaulle airport ( pictured ) . the heist took place in the landy tunnel , which is just under a mile long and notorious for smash-and-grabs .
