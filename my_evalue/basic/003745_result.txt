chelsea midfielder eden hazard was the toast of the english game as he carried off the pfa player of the year award . the 24-year-old belgian has scored 18 goals across all competitions for his club this season , helping the blues win the capital one cup and putting them on the brink of a first premier league title since 2010 . hazard has remarkably won player of the year or young player of the year every season since 2009 with lille or chelsea .
