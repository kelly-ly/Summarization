in this december 2014 file photograph , girls dance to fiddle music at twin sisters dance hall in blanco , texas . a preservation group says old texas dance halls that for years served as important social centers in rural areas of the state are decaying and closing , with comparatively few still operating . the culture has shifted away from rural areas where most of the halls are , but what we see is that these cultures are foundational to what texans are and what texas is , ' said patrick sparks , board president of texas dance hall preservation .
