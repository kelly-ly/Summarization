pregnant women who suffer extreme morning sickness are at greater risk of their children having language and speech delays , and attention disorders , warn researchers . a new study shows the condition suffered by the duchess of cambridge during both her pregnancies triples the risk of developmental problems compared with women who have normal nausea and vomiting . the condition hyperemesis gravidarum ( hg ) – which the duchess of cambridge has suffered during both her pregnancies – affects about one in 100 expectant mothers a year .
