a decorated marine has been considered ' unfit ' to re-enlist after getting a rifle tattoo on his forearm to commemorate his previous deployment . sgt . daniel knapp from jacksonville , north carolina , received combat medals after serving in afghanistan , but has been denied re-enlistment due to the service 's strict policy on body ink . while the army has recently updated its policy , the marine corps introduced more stringent rules in 2010 concerning the size and placement of tattoos that knapp has fallen foul of .
