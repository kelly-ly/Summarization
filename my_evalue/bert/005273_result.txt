a girl who believed she had a bad hangover was found to have 13 blood clots in her lungs . she rushed to hospital , where doctors found 13 pulmonary embolisms , blood clots blocking the main artery of her lungs , killing the tissue and stopping her from breathing properly . she has given up drinking and taken up exercise in an effort to lead the healthiest life possible , and wants to encourage other to do the same . the shock of being diagnosed twice has meant she has decided to turn her life around .
