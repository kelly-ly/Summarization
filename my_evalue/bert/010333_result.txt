eleven football fans have had their death sentences upheld after a retrial over a stadium riot in egypt which left 74 people dead . an appeals court ordered the retrial of 73 defendants last year after rejecting a lower court 's verdict , which sentenced 21 people to death . a year later , dozens of people also died in the canal city during riots which started after the lower court handed down the 21 original death sentences . the authorities reacted by imposing a ban on fans attending matches and held games behind closed doors .
