( cnn ) five militants from the kurdistan workers ' party were killed and another was wounded in clashes with turkish armed forces in eastern turkey , the country 's military said saturday . the kurdish separatists opened fire from a long distance , targeting turkish soldiers who were securing an area in agri ahead of a spring festival , cnn turk reported . about a fifth of turkey 's population is kurdish -- a minority long living under cultural oppression , most of them in the underdeveloped southeastern part of turkey .
