( cnn ) sabra dipping co. is recalling 30,000 cases of hummus due to possible contamination with listeria , the u.s. food and drug administration said wednesday . the potential for contamination was discovered when a routine , random sample collected at a michigan store on march 30 tested positive for listeria monocytogenes . listeria monocytogenes can cause serious and sometimes fatal infections in young children , frail or elderly people , and others with weakened immune systems , the fda says .
