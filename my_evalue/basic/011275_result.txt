the 200-year-old skeleton of a soldier killed at the battle of waterloo has been discovered under a car park - just like richard iii - and identified by archaeologists . brandt , a member of george iii’s german legion , was killed by napoleon’s forces and was found with a musket ball between his ribs , the sunday times reported . the team at waterloo had to use the process of elimination to identify their unknown soldier and could not rely on dna tests like those used by the group that discovered richard iii under a car park in leicester in 2012 .
