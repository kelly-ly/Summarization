( cnn ) percy sledge , the r & b belter whose biggest hit , `` when a man loves a woman , '' became a cornerstone of soul music , died tuesday . over a mournful , slowly rising instrumental track provided by organist spooner oldham , drummer roger hawkins and guitarist marlin greene -- key musicians of what became the muscle shoals sound , heard on countless soul records -- sledge crooned , pleaded and roared his way through the tune . he had some regrets about that , he told blues and soul magazine -- the song would have helped provide for his children -- but he accepted it .
