few people can fail be wowed by technicolor butterfly wings that ' sparkle ' in the sunlight . as well as the beautiful colours , they show the layout of individual scales , which give the insect its iridescence . each scale is pigmented with melanins that give butterflies black and brown colouring , but eye-catching blues , greens , reds and iridescence are created by the microstructure of the scales , rather than pigments themselves . the ability of monarch butterflies to detect ultraviolet light helps them on their annual migration from north america to mexico , for example .
