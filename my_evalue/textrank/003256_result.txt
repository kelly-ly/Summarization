apple watch uses green led lights paired with light‑sensitive photodiodes to detect the amount of blood flowing through your wrist at any given moment . ' when your heart beats , the blood flow in your wrist - and the green light absorption - is greater . between beats , it’s less . ' by flashing its led lights hundreds of times per second , apple watch can calculate the number of times the heart beats each minute - your heart rate . ' two out of the three models of apple watches have sapphire glass screens - the apple watch and apple watch edition .
