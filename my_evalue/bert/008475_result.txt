a 16-year-old who was born a girl but identifies as a boy has been granted the opportunity to go through male puberty after years of living in misery . the queensland teen is just one of many young australians who have to seek permission from the family court and prove they are making an ‘independent’ choice before they can be granted second-stage gender-changing hormones . currently a child wishing to change gender has to apply to the family court for this second stage of treatment after they have first taken puberty blocking hormones .
