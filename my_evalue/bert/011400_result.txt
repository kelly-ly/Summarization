a 200-year-old book of poetry gifted to george iii and letters from rear admiral sir samuel hood written during the american war of independence are among a treasure trove of documents released by royal archive yesterday . other papers include an essay written by george iii on the relationship between the monarch and parliament and notes from queen charlotte to her children 's governess . the digitisation of the 350,000 documents , a task that will be undertaken by academics from king 's college london , will begin in the next few weeks . the collection also includes 65,000 books from the library amassed by george iii which , along with volumes of poetry , also include a number printed in and about the us - testament to the monarch 's interest in the colony .
