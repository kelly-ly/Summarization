west ham striker diafra sakho is out for the remainder of the season after tearing a thigh muscle on saturday against stoke city . the 25-year-old is west ham 's runaway top scorer this season with 12 goals and was forced off after 59 minutes during their 1-1 draw at home . west ham claimed sakho could return ' before the end of the season ' on their official website , but it appears unlikely the senegal international will be fit enough to feature before the next campaign .
