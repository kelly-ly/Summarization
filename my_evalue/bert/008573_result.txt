this is a shocking collection of harrowing photographs of the death and destruction visited on armenians by ottoman turks a century ago that were taken by an american and a colleague who risked imprisonment to smuggle them out and show the world the full horror of what happened . john elder and armin t. wegner both documented the unimaginable suffering they witnessed in images which helped build a case against a turkish government which still denies the slaughter of up to 1.5million armenians constituted genocide . the image was taken in 1915 just before the mass deportation of armenians to the desert had begun . around 10,000 are thought to have been led here by turkish military police in 1915 before being brutally murdered and thrown into the cleft in the rock .
