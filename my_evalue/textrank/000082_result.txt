( cnn ) sabra dipping co. is recalling 30,000 cases of hummus due to possible contamination with listeria , the u.s. food and drug administration said wednesday . the potential for contamination was discovered when a routine , random sample collected at a michigan store on march 30 tested positive for listeria monocytogenes . although some people may suffer only short-term symptoms such as high fever , severe headache , nausea , abdominal pain and diarrhea , listeria can also cause miscarriages and stillbirths among pregnant women .
