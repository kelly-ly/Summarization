three reportedly inexperienced abalone divers died after jumping into rough seas off the coast of northern california on sunday . witnesses said at least some of the men appeared to be inexperienced abalone divers who chose rough , choppy waters near several dangerous rock outcroppings to dive - instead of entering the water at the soft beach nearby . firefighters said the men dove into water in caspar cove and found themselves immediately in trouble . the three men who drowned were overcome by the churning water and crushed against the sharp rocks on shore .
