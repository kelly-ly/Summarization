( billboard ) considering the academy of country music awards celebrated its 50th anniversary on sunday night at the dallas cowboys stadium , it was bound to be bigger than any previous year 's acms . plus , as hosts blake shelton and luke bryan were quick to point out , everything is bigger in texas . despite some mild controversy over their song `` girl crush , '' little big town brought the poignant ballad to the awards show , giving the acms one of its more melancholy moments . taylor swift was given an extended honor at this year 's acm awards , but some of the milestone awards -- especially those given to reba mcentire , kenny chesney and george strait -- seemed rushed for the country giants they were saluting .
