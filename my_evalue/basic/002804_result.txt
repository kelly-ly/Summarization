blackburn boss gary bowyer was in ' shock ' as he watched his goalkeeper simon eastwood almost snatch a last-gasp draw for his side against liverpool . trailing 1-0 following philippe coutinho 's 70th-minute strike , eastwood left his post guarding the blackburn goal and arrived in the liverpool penalty area in a bid to help his side draw level with seconds of the contest remaining . bowyer had looked ready to celebrate the latest of equalisers after eastwood skilfully controlled the ball before testing his reds counterpart simon mignolet with a tricky drive .
