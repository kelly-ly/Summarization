deep within utah 's san rafael desert , thousands upon thousands of goblins lurk in the shadows . once known as the mushroom valley , the area was first discovered by cowboys searching for cattle . in 1964 it was designated a state park . the site also boasts some of the darkest skies in the world , and park rangers offer a tour showcasing the night sky , gazing upon stars , planets and distant galaxies , learning about the stories behind the constellations .
