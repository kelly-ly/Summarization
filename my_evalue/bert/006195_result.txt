hearts have confirmed that captain danny wilson will leave the club this summer after the defender activated a clause in his contract . hearts head coach robbie neilson said : ‘danny’s decided he wants a new challenge and , as i’ve said previously , i’ll never stand in the way of any player who wants to move on so he goes with our blessing and our thanks . ‘ hearts manager robbie neilson ( left ) admits it will be difficult to lose the influential defender . wilson is suspended for this weekend’s clash with cowdenbeath but is set to lift the championship trophy on may 2 as hearts wrap up the campaign at home to rangers .
