( cnn ) police in india are putting aside their batons in favor of an overhead solution to angry and unruly crowds : pepper-spraying drones . yashasvi yadav , senior superintendent of police in lucknow , northern india , told cnn the city 's force has bought four drones and is in the process of purchasing one more . protests are a common occurrence in india , a country with a population of 1.2 billion ; lucknow , the capital of the northern state of uttar pradesh , also used drone cameras to monitor crowds at a recent religious festival .
