serena williams claimed her eighth miami open title in 14 years after ruthlessly brushing aside the challenge of 12th seed carla suarez navarro in saturday 's final . williams , who earlier this week claimed her 700th career victory , never looked back after breaking suarez navarro in the sixth game of a one-sided final . the opening five games had gone with serve but williams moved up a gear and from then on she never looked back . suarez navarro , who beat williams ' sister venus in the quarter-final , saved the first two break points but a wayward backhand handed the american the first of five breaks of serve .
