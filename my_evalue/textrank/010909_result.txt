ancestors : joseph getty is great-grandson of j. paul getty , left , and grandson of sir john paul getty , right . the getty dynasty was started by j. paul getty , joseph 's great-grandfather , who became the richest man in the world thanks to the getty oil company and went on to set up a number of acclaimed art institutions , despite being a notorious miser . sir john paul getty , who died in 2003 , was an anglophile who became a british citizen and built a replica of the oval cricket ground at his country estate in buckinghamshire , wormsley park .
