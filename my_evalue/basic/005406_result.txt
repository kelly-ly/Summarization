juan mata has collected his player of the month award for march from manchester united and was quick to thank his supporters after receiving the gong . mata scored both goals as united overturned liverpool with a 2-1 win at anfield , while also producing an impressive display in the 3-0 home victory over tottenham . the spain international 's return to the united side coincided with two of the best performances from louis van gaal 's team this season against spurs and liverpool and the fans were quick to acknowledge mata 's influence .
