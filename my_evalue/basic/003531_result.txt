manchester city have apologised to supporters and promised to hand out ticket refunds after coaches they laid on for free were delayed and missed the kick-off at crystal palace . palace 's south london home is notoriously difficult to get to and the club acted after the game was scheduled for an 8pm start , making it impossible for supporters to travel by train . however , after being sent on their way from the etihad stadium at 1.30pm by city legend mike summerbee , all of those onboard the six coaches missed the kick-off with some arriving as much as 20 minutes after the start .
