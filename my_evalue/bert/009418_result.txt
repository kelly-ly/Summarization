china have said they are to start tracking the actions of their citizens abroad , following a string of reports about behaviour abroad . the china national tourism administration ( cnta ) said on its website on monday that provincial and national authorities will be in touch with unruly citizens when they return to china , and if necessary , the police , customs officers , border control and even bank credit agencies will be contacted . these figures are a dramatic increase from the 10 million outbound tourists in 2000 , and reflected the rise in numbers of reports on disruptive behaviour . in december a brawl started on a chinese flight from to chongqing to hong kong over a woman 's crying baby .
