( cnn ) pope francis reminded the world of the vatican 's status as a state and his role as a moral diplomat in his traditional speech given at the end of easter mass . `` may the international community not stand by before the immense humanitarian tragedy unfolding in these countries and the drama of the numerous refugees , '' he said in his `` urbi et orbi '' address , latin for `` to the city ( rome ) and to the world .
