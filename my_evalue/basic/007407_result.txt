a year after he launched his own app and made headlines , leo grand is still homeless . in august 2013 mcconlogue offered grand , the homeless man he saw every day on his walk to work in new york city , a deal : he would either give him $ 100 , or he would teach him how to code . mcconlogue provided grand with daily lessons , as well as a laptop , wireless hot spot , coding books , a solar charger and office space at the start-up where he worked once it got too cold to type outside .
