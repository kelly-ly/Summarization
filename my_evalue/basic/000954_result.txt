( cnn ) anthony doerr 's `` all the light we can not see , '' a novel centered on the world war ii bombing of st.-malo , france , and two characters on opposite sides of the war , won the pulitzer prize for fiction monday . `` i must blame anthony doerr for lost sleep , because once i started reading his new novel , ' all the light we can not see , ' there was no putting it down , '' wrote william t. vollmann in the new york times book review . doerr 's work was also a finalist for the national book award .
