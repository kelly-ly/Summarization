experts have discovered that this dove grey suit was once worn by notorious nazi leader hermann goering . goering , who founded the gestapo and was commander of the german air force , was well known for being overweight and as a result the suit shows the strain of being worn by the large officer . there was also an effort by goering to appear less grand during the latter part of the war . goering was indicted by the international military tribunal in nuremberg in 1946 .
