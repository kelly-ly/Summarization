angela merkel threw down the gauntlet to david cameron last night and said large countries should be prepared to accept more asylum seekers . the german chancellor demanded a new european union system that distributes asylum-seekers to member states based on their population and economic strength . he told his counterparts at the eu summit that the uk is already ' playing its part ' in the region through the aid budget and offer of ships , and takes in its ' fair share ' of refugees compared to other member states .
