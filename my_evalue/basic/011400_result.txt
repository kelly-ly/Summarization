a 200-year-old book of poetry gifted to george iii and letters from rear admiral sir samuel hood written during the american war of independence are among a treasure trove of documents released by royal archive yesterday . other papers include an essay written by george iii on the relationship between the monarch and parliament and notes from queen charlotte to her children 's governess . the viewing took place in the royal library at windsor castle and include papers owned and written by george i , ii , iii and george iv , who ruled as prince regent during his father 's famous madness .
