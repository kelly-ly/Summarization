a small mistake from lewis hamilton allowed mercedes team-mate nico rosberg to gain the upper hand in second practice for the bahrain grand prix . nico rosberg ( pictured ) got the better of mercedes team-mate lewis hamilton in bahrain second practice . that was due to a lock-up from hamilton in the second sector of the bahrain international circuit which cost him four tenths of a second , otherwise he would likely have beaten rosberg by three tenths given he was faster in sectors one and two .
