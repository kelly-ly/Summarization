the 100th anniversary of the landing of anzac troops at gallipoli during world war i has been commemorated by the creation of a freshly minted $ 2 coin . it also features a small circle that is the colour of red poppy flowers . an artist 's illustration shows the symbolic red poppy design on the new $ 2 coin to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the gallipoli landing by the anzacs . there will be about one and a half million coins released into circulation over the coming weeks , as part of a series of commemorative coins that capture the history , service and sacrifice of australians at war .
