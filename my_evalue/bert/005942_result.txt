borussia dortmund have moved swiftly to secure thomas tuchel as the successor to jurgen klopp , after he announced he was bringing the curtain down on his seven-year stay at the club this week . like klopp , the 41-year-old tuchel carved a name for himself at german club mainz . in the same interview , tuchel commended the work and achievements of bayern munich boss pep guardiola , the man he will now look to lock horns with as dortmund manager . tuchel admitted that spanish and english football is of interest to him but he wanted to return to management in germany .
