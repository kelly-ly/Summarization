( cnn ) canadian actor jonathan crombie , who co-starred in the `` anne of green gables '' tv movies , died this week at age 48 . crombie died wednesday from complications of a brain hemorrhage , `` anne of green gables '' producer kevin sullivan said . based on canadian author lucy maud montgomery 's children 's books , `` anne of green gables '' debuted in canada on cbc tv in 1984 and became a cultural touchstone . as one person said on twitter , `` i do n't know any female canadian from my generation that *didn't* have at least a little bit of a crush on jonathan crombie as gilbert . '' crombie went on to play roles in other american and canadian tv shows , including `` 21 jump street '' and `` the good wife , '' but even his facebook page acknowledges he is best known for his portrayal of gilbert blythe .
