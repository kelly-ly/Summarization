kevin pietersen has thrown his support behind former team-mate michael vaughan as the right man to ' change the brand of cricket currently being played ' in england . ten years on from his career-defining captaincy in the 2005 ashes , vaughan is the hot favourite to be appointed as director of england cricket , a streamlined version of the managing director 's job which was abolished with paul downton 's sacking on wednesday . the 40-year-old has previously kept his distance from a formal role with the ecb , preferring to focus on his burgeoning media profile and business interests , but has declared himself open to discussions about the new post .
