at the grand old age of 75 , jack nicklaus is still capable of hitting aces . the golden bear added another magic moment to his storied career at augusta national in the par-3 contest . stepping up to the tee on the 130-yard fourth , the greatest golfer of all time saw his shot sail beyond the flag before spinning back into the hole . nicklaus became the youngest person to wear a green jacket in 1963 , and collected his sixth in 1986 .
