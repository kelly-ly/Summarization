in recent years , many companies have embraced open offices with about 70 per cent of u.s. however , a 2013 study revealed that workers are frustrated by the distractions in open offices which lead to poorer work performance , according to the washington post . that study also found that nearly half of the surveyed workers in open offices said the lack of sound privacy proved to be a problem for them while more than 30 per cent complained about the lack of visual privacy .
