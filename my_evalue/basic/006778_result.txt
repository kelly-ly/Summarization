the leaders of places with names like molossia , westarctica , vikesland and broslavia are coming together at the anaheim central library for microcon 2015 , which has been dubbed the first north american gathering of micronations . molossia , population five , is just one of the small , self-proclaimed micronations that will be represented at microcon 2015 in los angeles this weekend . founded in albuquerque by grand duke jacob felts , whose name inspired the nation 's capital of feltasia , broslavia has only five citizens - but its own volunteer military .
