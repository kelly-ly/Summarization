these rusting ' ghost ' ships are the last remnants of what was once the united states ' national defense reserve fleet , set to protect and serve in the hour of need . ghost ships : a number of the remaining ships in the united states ' national defense reserve fleet lay moored in james river , virginia . at its height in 1950 , the ndrf consisted of 2,277 ships at eight anchorage sites : james river , virginia ; beaumont , texas ; suisun bay , california ; stony point , new york ; wilmington , north carolina ; mobile , alabama ; astoria , oregon ; and olympia , washington .
