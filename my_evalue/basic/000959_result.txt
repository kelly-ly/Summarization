( cnn ) nobel literature laureate guenter grass , best known around the world for his novel `` the tin drum , '' has died , his publisher said monday . grass died in a clinic in the city of luebeck , where he was taken over the weekend , said steidl publishing spokeswoman claudia glenewinkel . three years ago , grass drew controversy again , when he published a poem in a german newspaper discouraging germany from selling more submarines to israel .
