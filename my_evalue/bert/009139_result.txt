a scientist has warned britain is facing a collapse in society similar to ancient rome - because we 've lived too peacefully for too long . australian historian dr jim penman claims britons today have lost the drive for innovation that epitomised the victorian era as we enjoy a more comfortable standard of living . we live in a golden age where there have been no major wars in europe for three quarters of a century . dr penman , of the rmit university in melbourne , made the claims in his book ' biohistory : decline and fall of the west ' , and adds that christianity helped drive social change in britain which transformed the roles of women and children .
