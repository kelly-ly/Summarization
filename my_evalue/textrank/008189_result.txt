tomas berdych advanced to the semi-finals of the monte carlo masters for the third time on friday after opponent milos raonic retired with a foot injury . berdych , who has yet to lose a set this week , will face either grigor dimitrov or gael monfils in the semi-finals of the clay-court masters tournament . eight-time champion rafael nadal will play david ferrer , and top-ranked novak djokovic will face marin cilic .
