wayne rooney has revealed how he is developing his own style of captaincy to lead manchester united this season . rooney has served under a number of big names for club and country during his career , but admits that he has had to learn to be his own man after louis van gaal handed him the armband last summer . last weekend’s emphatic derby win over manchester city was their sixth premier league victory in a row as van gaal’s side head towards a top-four finish .
