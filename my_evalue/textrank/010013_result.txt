an australian festival organiser has slammed tasmania as a state full ' dregs , bogans and third-generation morons ' . mr schofield moved to hobart to set up a baroque music festival , but decided to relocate back to new south wales after the tasmanian government cut the festivals funding by 25 per cent . after receiving an offer from queensland arts organisations he decided to move the festival to brisbane . while mr schofield admits he was ' absolutely obsessed , tremendously intrigued ' by tasmania , he continued to blast tasmanian 's for wanting to ' dig it up , chop it down , sell it to the chinese . ' luke martin from the tourism industry council of tasmania tweeted the above after mr schofield said of tasmania : ' all the young people leave , and the only ones left are the dregs , the bogans , the third-generation morons ' .
