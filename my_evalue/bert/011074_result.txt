a new study has shown women still have an increased risk of breast cancer years after stopping taking hormone replacement therapy . after going through the menopause a woman 's ovaries stop producing the hormone oestrogen . hrt treats the body by introducing female sex hormones . the new study , led by dr rowan chlebowski , an oncologist at los angeles biomedical research institute , and one of the initial investigators on whi , focused on a woman 's risk of breast cancer after stopping hrt treatment .
