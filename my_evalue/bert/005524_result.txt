inspired by the post-impressionistic era of the belle epoque , the newly-opened maison souquet may just be the most provocative hotel in paris . considered by many to be the most romantic city in the world , the city of lights ' latest overnight offering takes a page from the maisons closes , the turn-of-the-century pleasures houses that were frequented by socialites and aesthetes . the 20-room hotel describes itself as a ' temple to hedonism , ' and the property 's butlers are on hand to assist guests with everything and anything they desire . but it 's the property 's celestial spa that may just be its most impressive .
