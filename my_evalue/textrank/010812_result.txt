nasa has unveiled a revolutionary type of wing that it says could save millions in air travel . figures suggest the breakthrough could increase fuel efficiency up to 12 per cent , and reduce noise at take-off and landing up to 40 per cent , compared to traditional wing flaps ( one shown on a boeing 737 ) . the skyprowler has four horizontal propellers that allow it to perform vertical take-offs and landings , and to hover in mid-air .
