the great dying , which struck the earth 252 million years ago , wiped out more than 96 per cent of the world 's creatures . the great dying , also known as the permian-triassic boundary extinction , took place over a 60,000-year period , and acidification of the oceans lasted for around 10,000 years . huge volumes of viscous basalt lava covered an area roughly seven times the size of france , explained the natural history museum , and sulphur dioxide from these eruptions caused vinegar-like acid rain to cover the earth .
