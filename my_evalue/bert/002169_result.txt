david cameron today boasted that 2million people have found work since 2010 , as new employment figures showed the uk workforce is at another record high . the office for national statistics ( ons ) said that more than 31 million people were in work after an increase of more than half a million in the past year , the biggest total since records began in 1971 . the number of people claiming jobseeker 's allowance fell by 20,700 in march to 772,400 , the 29th consecutive monthly cut . youth unemployment - 16 to 24-year-olds - fell by 22,000 in the three months to february to 742,000 .
