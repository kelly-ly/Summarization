the first dramatic footage of the hatton garden jewellery heist shows how the gang returned twice in three days to continue their audacious robbery - as it is revealed police may have to pay out millions in compensation for ignoring the alarm . images obtained by the daily mirror reveal the precise timings of the raid for the first time - as well as the fact they used wheelie bins to remove millions of pounds worth of jewellery and valuables from the safe . the footage begins at 9.23pm – just four minutes after staff at the safety deposit centre lock up for the easter weekend – as the first thief appears on camera carrying a black bin bag . another suspect , riding a moped , may also be connected to the gang .
