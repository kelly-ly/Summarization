nhs bosses have today come under fire after it emerged a hospital paid a temporary nurse £2,200 to cover a single 12-hour shift . the shrewsbury and telford hospital nhs trust paid £2,200 to a temporary nurse for one 12-hour shift to help amid a shortage of trained nurses , a freedom of information request revealed ( file picture ) . defending their spending , sarah bloomfield , director of nursing at shrewsbury and telford hospital nhs trust , said temporary staff had to be used to cover a shortfall in trained nurses .
