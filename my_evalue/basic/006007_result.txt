the owner of the bunny baker cafe in manila etches customised caricatures into coffee froth at no extra cost to his clientele , even detailing local favourite , boxer manny pacquiao . graphic artist zach yonzon runs the cafe with his wife and uses steamed milk and froth as the canvas upon which he creates his masterpieces which can leave happy memories for tourists . captured in the short video , the cafe 's owner mr yonzon can be seen etching the face of the much-loved filipino boxer manny pacquiao , into a cup of coffee .
