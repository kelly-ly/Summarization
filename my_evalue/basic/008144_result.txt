the woman who was tragically killed when her car washed away in flood waters in maitland has been identified as an elderly great-grandmother who was popping out for milk . the body of anne jarmain , 86 , was found at 7pm on wednesday ; 10 hours after her car was pulled off the road and underwater as she tried to crossed cessnock road , which in the nsw hunter region . ms jarmain was dragged into the waters when she tried to manoeuvre her silver hatchback around a stalled falcon before 9am on wednesday .
