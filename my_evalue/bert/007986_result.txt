downton abbey fans left reeling that the hit show is coming to an end can rejoice – for the sharp-tongued dowager countess is among a clutch of characters who may return to our screens . played by dame maggie smith in the itv drama , violet , countess of grantham may be coming back in the new american show the gilded age , from downton creator julian fellowes . there may also be a place for robert , the earl of grantham , and his wife cora , who is american-born . nbc president robert greenblatt told a meeting of tv critics he hoped the gilded age would ‘ come to life sometime next season’ – which could mean it is on air as early as september .
