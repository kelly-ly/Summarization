( cnn ) thursday will mark three weeks since saudi arabia began airstrikes on houthi rebels in yemen . but there is as yet little sign that the rebels are being driven back , that the fighting in yemen is dying down or that lives there are being saved . the u.n. security council voted tuesday in favor of an arms embargo on houthis -- the minority group that has taken over large swaths of yemen , including its capital , sanaa -- and supporters of former yemeni president ali abdullah saleh .
