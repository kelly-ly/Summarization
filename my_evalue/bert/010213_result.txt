one of the less celebrated aspects of richie benaud’s remarkable career were his regular appearances in that part of the press box which houses the written journalists . but benaud also penned a regular column for the news of the world , taking his place among the hacks – not all tv men take the trouble – to craft his copy whenever a test match was going on . benaud did his best to change all that . surely , you wondered , he must have felt strongly about subjects other than the front-foot no-ball law .
