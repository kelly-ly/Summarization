david cameron will today announce that all children who do not pass their tests aged 11 will have to take them again in the first year of secondary school , when they are 12 . secondary schools currently receive a £500 ‘catch-up premium’ for any child who arrives without having met the expected standard by the time they leave primary school . the resit tests will be slimmed down literacy and numeracy checks containing content from the assessments taken in the last year of primary school , as well as some content from the curriculum of the first year in secondary school .
