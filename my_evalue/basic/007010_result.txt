the overbearing and interfering mother-in-law is often jokingly portrayed as most people’s least favourite family member . and now a new poll on modern families reveals this is a reality for almost a quarter of britons – who said their pet dog was more important to them than their in-laws . of the 2,071 people surveyed , 22 per cent cited their pets as a close family member , above in-laws ( 21 per cent ) but below grandparents at 26 per cent .
