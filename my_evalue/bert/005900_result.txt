this too-close-for-comfort video shows a swirling ' landspout ' tornado tearing up the texas countryside from just 100 yards away . daredevil stan cole , an iraq veteran , captured the wall of wind as it gulped up dust from the fields in loraine , western texas . he said : ' perhaps 10 years in iraq and afghanistan , my danger meter needs to be recalibrated ' . landspouts - the scientific term for tornadoes which are not part of a thunderstorm - can be difficult to capture on film .
