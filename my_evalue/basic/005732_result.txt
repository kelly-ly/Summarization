the eshima ohashi bridge in japan – the third largest of its kind in the world – rises sharply so ships can pass underneath . the concrete road bridge spans a mile across lake nakaumi , linking the cities of matsue and sakaiminato . the two-lane concrete road bridge stretches 1.7km or just over 1 mile long and has a width of 11.4 metres making it the largest rigid frame bridge in japan and the third largest in the world .
