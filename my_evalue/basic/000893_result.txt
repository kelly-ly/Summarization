kathmandu , nepal ( cnn ) rescue crews and residents in nepal early sunday began the desperate search for survivors after a magnitude-7.8 quake near the capital of kathmandu a day earlier flattened homes , buildings and temples , causing widespread damage across the region and killing more than 1,800 people . `` the reports of the devastation are still coming in and the numbers of people killed , injured and affected by this earthquake continue to rise , '' said u.n. secretary-general ban ki-moon in a statement . an estimated 4.6 million people in the region were exposed to tremors from the nepal earthquake , the united nations office for the coordination of humanitarian affairs said via twitter .
