mexican restaurant chipotle has been leaving customers hungry for certain ingredients unsatisfied because not enough farms can meet its strict standards . farmers are wary of the increased production cost for premium , antibiotic-free pork and worry about finding people to pay an appropriate price for the ' premium ' meat after denver-based chipotle has the bits it wants . as of thursday morning , its stock price had fallen more than five points since news of the continued shortage was published wednesday . the restaurant has also warned of possible guacamole shortages .
