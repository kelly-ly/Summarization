more people in britain back fracking than are opposed to it , a greenpeace survey has found . the greenpeace survey , carried out by comres , found greatest support among men ( 56 per cent ) and the over-65s ( 58 per cent ) . at least 35 of the seats being targeted in the tories ' election strategy are in areas licensed for fracking , as are 11 labour and eight lib dem seats held with a swing of two per cent or less . fracking , or hydraulic fracturing , is a method of extracting natural gas from rock deep underground by blasting it with a mixture of water , sand and chemicals .
