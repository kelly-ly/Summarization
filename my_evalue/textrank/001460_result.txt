west brom have honoured the memory of former striker jeff astle on ' astle day ' against leicester city in saturday 's premier league encounter . the baggies are commemorating the launch of the jeff astle foundation and are wearing a replica of their 1968 fa cup winning kit , where astle scored the winner against everton . saido berahino was wearing no 9 for west brom , formerly worn by west brom icon astle .
