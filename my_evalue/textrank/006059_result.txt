aidan turner has been signed up for a second series of bbc period drama poldark . the series , based on winston graham’s novels , follows ross poldark after he returns from the american war of independence only to find that his father has died and his former fiancée , elizabeth , has married another man . the first series of poldark , written by debbie horsfield , covers the first of mr graham’s two novels , named after ross poldark and demelza .
