four days after klopp announced he believes he has taken the club as far as he can and is seeking pastures new , dortmund have revealed tomas tuchel will take over in july on a three-year deal . like klopp , the 41-year-old tuchel carved a name for himself at german club mainz . in the same interview , tuchel commended the work and achievements of bayern munich boss pep guardiola , the man he will now look to lock horns with as dortmund manager .
