a grandmother is facing a raft of serious charges after being involved in a severe car crash and failing a sobriety test at the crime scene . patricia ebel , 49 , was driving her 10-year-old grandson home after a day at the pool on friday when she crashed her luxury black bmw 5-series into a car that was stopped at an intersection in naples , florida . ebel is facing dui charges including driving with a .15-or-greater blood alcohol content with a minor in the car . she also refused to take a breathalyzer test .
