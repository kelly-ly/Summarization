this graphic above shows the influx of genes to europe that brought lighter skin colour and taller body shapes , according to the new research on ancient human remains conducted by geneticists at harvard university . it was not until 7,800 years ago , when the first farmers migrated from the near east through turkey that two key genes that provide lighter skin appeared . two of the genes were associated with producing light skin - slc24a5 and slc45a2 .
