( cnn ) the red cross on saturday called for an immediate 24-hour ceasefire in battle-torn yemen , saying many more people recently wounded in airstrikes and ground fighting will die if not tended to soon . yemen has been descending into chaos in the weeks since houthi rebels -- minority shiites who have long complained of being marginalized in the majority sunni country -- forced yemeni president abdu rabu mansour hadi from power in january . in aden alone , fighting has killed 58 people and injured 200 more in the past two days , yemeni security officials said .
