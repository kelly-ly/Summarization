nasa has unveiled a revolutionary type of wing that it says could save millions in air travel . the wing is able to morph itself like that of a bird to provide the same functionality as normal flaps , but with much less weight . the project is a collaboration between nasa , the air force research laboratory ( afrl ) and flexsys inc from ann arbor michigan . these flights cap 17 years of technology maturation , beginning with afrl’s initial phase 1 sbir contract with flexsys , and the technology now is ready to dramatically improve aircraft efficiency for the air force and the commercial aviation industry . ’
