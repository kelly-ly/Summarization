young women can still get cheaper car insurance than young men despite sex equality laws , an economist warned yesterday . insurers are dodging the rules that ban them from charging female drivers less by offering lower premiums to motorists who have jobs that are done mainly by women , he said . someone who tells their insurer they are a 21-year-old dental nurse is likely to get car insurance for 10 per cent less than they would have done in 2011 , he said .
