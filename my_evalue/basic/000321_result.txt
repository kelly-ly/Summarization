( cnn ) a door bearing a graffiti drawing by british artist banksy was seized by police in gaza on thursday after a dispute over its sale , a gaza police official told cnn on thursday . the owner of the door , rabea darduna , filed a complaint with a gaza court stating that , without realizing its value , he sold the door for just $ 175 u.s . darduna says he was duped into believing the door was a normal door when it was really a canvas for banksy , one of the world 's most famous graffiti artists , who had painted a greek goddess with her head in her hand .
