aeroplanes are infamous as breeding grounds for bacteria , with tray tables , seats and blankets singled out as the worst offenders on board . but an entrepreneurial team are hoping their new invention will revolutionise the cleaning of aircraft and help airlines win the battle against bacteria . last year a study from the auburn university in alabama discovered disease-causing bacteria can survive for up to a week inside plane cabins , on surfaces such as seat pockets , tray tables , window shades and armrests .
