celta vigo forward fabian orellana has been handed a one-match ban by the spanish football federation following his straight-red card for throwing grass at barcelona midfielder sergio busquets , the club has confirmed on their official website . naturally , busquets made the most of the contact and grabbed his eye , despite the grass clearly striking him on the back of the neck . orellana was shown a red card for throwing the chunk of grass in the direction of busquets . barcelona star dani alves attempts to talk with orellana following his red card .
