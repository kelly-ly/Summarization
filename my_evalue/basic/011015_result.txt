spfl clubs are poised to do battle over a late hibernian move to cash in on the premiership play-offs . under recently passed spfl rules , however , play-off sides must hand over 50 per cent of their play-off profits for distribution among lower league clubs within seven days . and , faced with the possible loss of substantial sums from two lucrative matches , hibs chairman rod petrie has garnered support from hearts and motherwell for a resolution to slash the profits levied from play-off finalists from 50 per cent to 25 per cent .
