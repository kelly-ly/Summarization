today , tall and bronzed , like a warrior from the legions of alexander the great , vassilis is at the wheel of a speedboat thrumming across the sparkling seas off the mount athos peninsula in north-eastern greece . vassilis is the boatman at the eagles palace , a long-established family hotel designed to look a little like one of the many monasteries further along the coast . it is 90 minutes ' drive from the airport at thessaloniki , along a road that meanders through olive groves and low-lying wooded valleys , into a corner of greece that is a world away from the fleshpots of the party islands far to the south .
