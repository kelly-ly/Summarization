ystradyfodwg , pronounced ust-rady-vod-ug , was the home of mrs clinton 's great grandmother - a woman who showed a determination , similar to that of the former first lady , to struggle through poverty and eventually emigrate to america with her family . mrs clinton 's great-grandmother mary griffiths was born in merthyr tydfil in the welsh valleys , and from an early age supported her widowed mother . it was in america that hannah jones - who was to become mrs clinton 's grandmother - met hugh rodham , part of the rodham family who had travelled to america two years earlier from northumberland .
