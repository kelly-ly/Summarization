a police officer has been charged with leaking confidential information about the arrest of fallen afl star ben cousins to his reporter girlfriend . police investigators became suspicious when ms dirksz ( right ) and her cameraman were the only people outside freemantle police station at 2am on march 12 when cousins ( left ) was granted bail . acting police commissioner steve brown said the policeman allegedly looked up the information on a police computer . mr brown said ms dirksz had done nothing wrong but told 6pr mornings radio mr jamieson’s alleged offences were an ‘ absolute breach of trust not only for this agency but for the community at large’ .
