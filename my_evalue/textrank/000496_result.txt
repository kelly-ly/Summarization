as protests erupt nationwide after the death of freddie gray , a black man who suffered a severe spinal cord injury while in police custody , some local businesses in baltimore are banding together to show their support for the city , while others are left to rebuild or repair after monday 's riot . it was solidarity for baltimore , for the `` black lives matter '' movement and for the local businesses that were destroyed during the rioting , the five-year baltimore resident explained . not only were students coming in for free lunches , but baltimore residents were coming by to see whether they could volunteer and offer their support .
