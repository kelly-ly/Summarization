martin vargic , a graphic designer from slovakia , has created a chart that shows how the most famous of the constellations have changed from 50,000 bc to 100,000ce . mr vargic said : ' on a short timescale , stars appear to maintain nearly fixed positions in relation to each other , long term observations show that all stars indeed move and all constellations gradually change over time . ' this animation shows how the constellation leo changed from 50,000bc to what it will look like in 100,000ce .
