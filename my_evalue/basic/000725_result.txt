dongguan , china ( cnn ) for a decade , the new south china mall -- the biggest shopping mall in the world -- has been an embarrassment for its owners and china . the mall spans five million square feet of shopping area , making it the largest in the world in terms of leasable space -- more than twice the size of mall of america , the biggest shopping center in the united states . ms shu , head of new south china mall 's marketing unit , told cnn that there are plans for a re-launch ceremony this spring , when all construction work will be completed and most shop and restaurant areas are leased to tenants .
