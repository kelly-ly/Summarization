it has been almost 20 years since a 21-year-old tiger woods stormed the course at augusta en route to his first victory at the masters in 1997 , and now there is a new american star on the block in the form of 2015 masters champion jordan spieth . the 21-year-old held a a four-shot lead heading into the final day of action and never looked back as he became the first man ever to reach 19 under par in the masters . having finished joint-second , along with jonas blixt , to bubba watson at last year 's tournament , spieth stormed to a final round of 70 to cap off an incredible few days on the georgia course .
