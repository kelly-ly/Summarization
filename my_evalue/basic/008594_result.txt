the dome-like hue go can be controlled wirelessly from any ios or android device via the philips hue app or any app developed for the lamp . when connected to the ' bridge ' , hue go can also be synced to music for a disco effect , as well as to television programmes , films and video games to create more of an atmosphere depending on the mood . mood lighting : the button has seven preset light settings : warm white light , cool daylight , and what philips describe as ' five natural dynamic effects ' that enhance moments such as meditation ( pictured ) .
