organic milk could contain a third less iodine than normal milk , scientists claim - but the organic milk industry has issued a swift rebuttal ( file picture ) . study author professor ian givens said the findings could have ‘ serious implications for public health’ . pregnant women and breastfeeding mothers who drink organic milk may be putting their child’s health at risk due to the smaller amount of iodine in the product . research in 2013 by experts in bristol and surrey found that children born to women deficient in iodine have a 60 per cent risk of lower iq scores at the age of nine .
