jose mourinho may have led chelsea to two premier league titles but his tally of three is not as many as the four won by joe kinnear during his days as wimbledon manager . remarkably , kinnear was manager of the month in september 1993 , march 1994 and then april 1994 as his team finished sixth before a final award in septmber 1996 . english managers dominate the list looking back , with 75 managers from this country having won the prize .
