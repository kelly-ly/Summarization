( cnn ) more than 500 houthi rebels have been killed since the start of saudi-led military operations against yemeni shia fighters , a saudi defense ministry official said saturday , according to the state-run saudi press agency . saudi forces responded to mortar rounds fired by houthis on a saudi border site . yemen has been descending into chaos in the weeks since houthi rebels -- minority shiites who have long complained of being marginalized in the majority sunni country -- forced yemeni president abdu rabu mansour hadi from power in january .
