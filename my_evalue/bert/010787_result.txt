coastlines : the story of our shore . popular television series explore it , gliding over the cliffs by helicopter as if in a dream . barkham’s book is written to celebrate 50 years of enterprise neptune , the national trust’s campaign to save our coastal beauties from being spoiled — by purchasing them . as a true coast-lover , barkham once proposed to a girlfriend by drawing the invitation in the sand and taking her up a dune to read it .
