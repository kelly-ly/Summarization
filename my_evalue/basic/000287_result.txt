besieged and bombed by syrian forces for more than two years , the desperate residents of this palestinian refugee camp near damascus awoke in early april to a new , even more terrifying reality -- isis militants seizing yarmouk after defeating several militia groups operating in the area . an estimated 18,000 refugees are now trapped inside yarmouk , stuck between isis and syrian regime forces in `` the deepest circle of hell , '' in the words of u.n. secretary-general ban ki-moon . activists and residents in yarmouk tell cnn that as many as 5,000 people have tried to flee their homes since isis stormed the camp , but have no place to go .
