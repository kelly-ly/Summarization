the miami open is threatening to turn into national cake week , after serena williams became the latest recipient of a giant celebration present after she clocked her 700th career victory . the day following andy murray’s 500th win another huge cake was wheeled out to mark williams reaching the semi-final with a 7-6 , 1-6 , 6-3 progression over germany’s sabine lisicki . williams’s hopes of another meeting with sister venus were dashed by her elder sibling’s defeat to spain’s carla suarez navarro , but the world no 1 was intent on staying alive in what is the nearest thing she has to a hometown event .
