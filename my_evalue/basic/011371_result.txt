a mummified monk who is believed to have lived for 102 years has become a major tourist attraction after he was put in a crystal display case sitting in the traditional lotus position of prayer . when wu yunqing passed away his disciples placed his body in the lotus position and sealed it inside a large porcelain vase with cement . earlier this year , mummified remains of a man apparently meditating in the lotus position were unearthed in mongolia with experts estimating the human relic was 200 years old .
