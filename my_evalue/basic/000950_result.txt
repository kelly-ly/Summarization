( cnn ) the man who beat serial killer jeffrey dahmer to death inside a wisconsin prison says he did it because of dahmer 's creepy sense of humor -- which included turning prison food into fake limbs covered with ketchup to resemble blood , the new york post reports . christopher scarver , who was sentenced to two life terms in prison for killing dahmer and another man at columbia correctional institution in 1994 , said the confessed cannibal would leave the fake limbs around the prison where others would find them . `` he crossed the line with some people -- prisoners , prison staff , '' the newspaper quoted scarver as saying .
