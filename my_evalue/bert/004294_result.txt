nestled between a 65ft high snow cavern , the winding track of murodo looks like track that could be on mario kart . however this steep-walled phenomena is located in japan and attracts visits from thousands of tourists annually - with the number predicted to rise this year . it is hoped that numbers can rise again to reach one million with the help of the sightseeing route operator hokuriku shinkansen line opening last month . snowy school trip !
