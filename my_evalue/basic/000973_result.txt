( cnn ) eternally blooming in kolkata , india , along the hooghly river is malik ghat , a wholesale flower market that attracts more than 2,000 sellers each day . photographer ken hermann visited the market for his project `` flower man , '' which is a series of portraits that casts light upon the people behind the petals . he said that in addition to language barriers and the heat of kolkata making communication complicated and shooting at certain times difficult , another adversity he faced was taking portraits of the female flower sellers .
