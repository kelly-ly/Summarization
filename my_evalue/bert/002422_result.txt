this incredible footage shows the moment an ' apocalyptic ' weather storm struck belarus , turning day into night when fast-moving storm clouds blocked out the sun . filmed from a high rise apartment , the video shows traffic moving along a busy street in the province of salihorsk , south of the capital of minsk on monday afternoon . towards the end of the video , the clouds thin out and the city once again slowly becomes illuminated . while sandstorms , or haboobs , are commonly associated with the sahara region , they can occur in any arid area .
