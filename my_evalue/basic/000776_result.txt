seoul ( cnn ) north korean leader kim jong un is continuing to rule with an iron fist , having ordered the execution of about 15 senior officials so far this year , according to an assessment by south korean intelligence agents , a lawmaker who attended a closed briefing said . shin kyung-min , a lawmaker with the new politics alliance for democracy , told a handful of reporters that he had been given the information by the south korean national intelligence service . the vice chairman of the state planning commission was executed because he objected to changing the design of a science and technology hall from a rounded shape to one resembling a flower , the intelligence officials said , according to the lawmaker .
