a florida community has voiced objection to plans of a forensic research ' body farm ' in which human bodies will be left in florida elements for extended periods of time . university of south florida , in a joint project with the hillsborough county sheriff 's office , have proposed building a ' body farm ' in lithia for research into how florida elements affect cadavers . erin kimmerle , associate professor of anthropology at usf , said that this research could potentially help solve the more than 500 cold cases in hillsborough , pasco and pinellas counties . it would operate solely on donations and grant funding .
