tottenham are confident kyle walker has not broken his foot following a scan on tuesday . there were fears the england defender may have fractured his right foot after taking a hefty blow from a collision with kieran trippier during the draw at burnley on sunday . he will continue to be assessed by spurs ' medical team in the coming days as the club determine the full extent of the injury . scans have revealed that walker has not broken his foot and will hopefully return to action for spurs soon .
