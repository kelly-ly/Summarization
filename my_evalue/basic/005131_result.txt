a tiny oil painting that was kept in an attic for years and thought to be worth just £300 has now sold for more than £120,000 at auction . the oil painting - which measures nine inches by seven - of a catholic saint was believed to be the work of a ' follower ' of 16th century renaissance master el greco . auctioneer richard bromell ( pictured ) gave the tiny painting an estimated value of about £300 to £400 but it ended up selling for more than £120,000 .
