the latest app that 's sweeping the web is the fiendishly addictive , and surprisingly difficult , kuku kube . although the game appears relatively simple , and starts with a board of just four coloured squares , it quickly grows to a board of up to 81 squares . there are eight levels , and as a player progresses the squares change orientation or add borders to make it harder . the colours of the pills are the same shade of grey , and the red and blue colours that people see are simply what their brains perceive , based on the rest of the information around them .
