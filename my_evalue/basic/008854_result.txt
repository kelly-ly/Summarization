william shakespeare may indeed have been the original author of the play double falsehood after all . the work titled double falsehood was presented by theatre impresario lewis theobald in the 18th century as an adaptation of a shakespeare play about a spanish nobleman 's ignoble pursuit of two women . theobald , a known scholar of shakespeare , mounted his play at drury lane theatre in london on december 13 , 1727 , claiming that it was a re-working of an original by the bard and that he had three original texts .
