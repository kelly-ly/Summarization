patrick bamford believes his long-term future lies at chelsea and hopes winning the championship player of the year award for his 19-goal season on loan at middlesbrough will strengthen his case . the striker beat ipswich town’s daryl murphy and watford’s troy deeney to win the prize at sunday night’s football league awards in central london . after previous loan spells at mk dons and derby county , the 21-year-old has produced his best form yet for aitor karanka’s promotion-chasing side .
