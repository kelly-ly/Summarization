along with her thoroughbred race horses , the queen also breeds fell ponies and has a stud specialising in highland ponies at balmoral . one of the oldest equine breeds on the planet , fell ponies like the queen 's mount carltonlima emma , have roamed the moors of cumbria since the neolithic period . docile , hardy and thick-set , the majority of fell ponies are black , grey or bay and measure between 13 and 14hh .
