the very first black james bond villain , yaphet kotto , has said the legendary secret agent role should only ever be portrayed by a white actor and ' political correctness be damned ' . kotto , who starred as dr kananga in the 1973 bond installment live and let die , said the role was created by ian fleming for a white actor and should be portrayed on the big screen as such . yaphet kotto ( pictured opposite roger moore in live and let die ) said james bond can not be portrayed by a black man .
