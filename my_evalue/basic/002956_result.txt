the first phase of disney 's polynesian villas & bungalows has just been unveiled , revealing opulent overwater villas available from $ 2,000 a night - rising to $ 4,500 at peak times . nestled along the shore of the seven seas lagoon , the resort is a quick monorail ride away from the theme park and also conveniently located close by to disney 's polynesian village resort . each bungalow reportedly sleeps up to eight people and offers a view of both the magic kingdom resort and the lagoon , where an electrical water pageant takes place each night .
