manchester city have already met jurgen klopp to assess him for the manager’s job – but it was back in 2013 when they were seeking a replacement for roberto mancini . txiki begiristain , city’s director of football met the borussia dortmund manager and concluded that he was not the right fit for city – and the club went on to appoint manuel pellegrini . begiristain’s meeting and rejection of klopp back in 2013 adds weight to the growing evidence that klopp will not get the manchester city job even though the borussia dortmund manager quit this week , fuelling a wave of speculation about which premier league clubs might want to appoint him this summer .
