a group of disabled manchester city supporters say they are ' disgusted ' with the club after they hiked prices of some season tickets by £345 to £975 . the 283 per cent rise , which comes after it emerged city were increasing the cost of some season tickets for able-bodied fans from £870 to £1,700 , has been strongly criticised by some of those affected . they claim the premier league champions are trying to force migration to other parts of the etihad stadium as part of a wide-ranging re-seating plan following a 6,000-seat extension which will see capacity rise to around 55,000 .
