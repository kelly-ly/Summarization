a london pub which survived relentless bombing during the blitz and was the only building in its road not destroyed by hitler 's bombs has been illegally knocked down to make way for luxury flats . the historic carlton tavern was bulldozed by developers without warning last week - just days before it was due to be marked as a listed building . the pub , which stood in maida vale , west london , was the only building in its road that was not destroyed by bombs during the blitz but did not survive cltx ltd , which was denied planning permission to turn it into flats by westminster city council in january .
