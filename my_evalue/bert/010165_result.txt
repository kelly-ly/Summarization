the milan derby used to be italian football’s biggest attraction . it was certainly the case in april 2005 when a hail of flares rained down from the stands on to the san siro pitch - flooding the stadium with smoke and forcing german referee markus merk to abandon a virulent champions league quarter-final . the 2005 clash – widely remembered for the chaotic scenes in which brazilian goalkeeper dida was knocked to the ground by a flare - was the second time in two seasons the sides had met in the knockout stages as the pair threatened continental dominance to add to their monopoly on serie a . 07/08 : 56,906 ( 1st ) .
