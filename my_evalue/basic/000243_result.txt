jakarta ( cnn ) an indonesian court has rejected a bid by two australian drug smugglers -- members of the `` bali nine '' -- to challenge their planned executions . andrew chan and myuran sukumaran are awaiting death by firing squad on indonesia 's `` execution island '' for their role in a failed 2005 heroin smuggling plot . a panel of three judges in the state administrative court in jakarta on monday confirmed an earlier ruling that it lacked the jurisdiction to hear challenges against president joko widodo 's refusal to grant clemency .
