manchester city star have met up with more than 100 local schoolchildren for the unveiling of a 64metre mural depicting ' the football effect ' at the club 's academy stadium . players including vincent kompany and gael clichy helped youngsters add the finishing touches to the design during a street party on wednesday - alongside women 's players lucy bronze and emma lipman and mathias bossaerts and kelechi iheanacho from the elite development squad . among the images featured are scenes of football being played around the world , including night football , street football and disabled leagues , while the club 's city football academy is at the centre of the design .
