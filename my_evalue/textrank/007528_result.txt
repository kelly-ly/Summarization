the former drummer and a founding member of the southern hard rock band lynyrd skynyrd , robert ' bob ' burns jr , died late friday in a single-vehicle crash near cartersville , georgia . burns ' vehicle went off the road just before midnight as it approached a curve , striking a mailbox and a tree , georgia state patrol spokeswoman tracey watson said . cartersville is about 125 miles away from macon , georgia , where duane allman died in a 1971 motorcycle crash .
