facebook is often linked with depression , social anxiety and other mental health issues . genes for good ( pictured ) is a facebook app designed to survey the health of millions of social network users , who can also send in spit samples to tie their survey results to their genes . it also keeps the dna . each member who submits a spit sample will also see a copy of their results , which include their genetic ancestry as well as raw genetic data in a securely encrypted computer file .
