train enthusiasts will be able to make the most of the beautiful moors scenery on board seven preserved engines form the golden age of steam and pistons . the north yorkshire moors railway was amongst 5,000 miles of track and 2,300 stations closed by dr richard beeching following his report released on march 27 , 1963 . however , it was one of the first to reopen , in 1973 , using old steam locomotives .
