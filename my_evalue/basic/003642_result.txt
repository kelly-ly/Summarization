the fierce rivalry between bayern munich and borussia dortmund has dominated german football for much of the past decade , reaching its crescendo when the two titans met in the 2013 champions league final at wembley . pierre-emerick aubameyang of borussia dortmund looks dejected after robert lewandowski of bayern munich scored an equaliser during der klassiker between the teams at the allianz arena in november . bayern munich defeated borussia dortmund 2-1 at wembley to win the champions league - a period in which der klassiker was one of the highlights on the european football calendar .
