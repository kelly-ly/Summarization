ex-juvenile offender shaun andrew mckerry , dubbed boomerang boy or homing pigeon boy in his youth , burst into shildon post office and stores , county durham , at 8pm on march 15 . blood can be seen coming from the would-be robber 's nose as mr dhillon gestures to his wife to drop the bat . mckerry , from coundon near bishop auckland , earned the nicknames boomerang boy or homing pigeon boy as he always returned home after committing a crime .
