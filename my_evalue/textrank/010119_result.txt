bbc trust chairman rona fairhead is facing calls to step down immediately as a director of hsbc over her links to the bank’s tax scandal . public accounts committee chairman , margaret hodge , told mrs fairhead she should resign or be sacked by the government as bbc trust chairman . last year mrs fairhead received more than £513,000 in fees and benefits for her work at the troubled bank , including a £334,000 fee as non-executive chairman of hsbc north america holdings .
