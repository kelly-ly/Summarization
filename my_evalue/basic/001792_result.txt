this fascinating interactive map shows where more than 231 million immigrants live around the world , and reveals that more than 7.8 million reside in the uk . official figures , released in february by the office for national statistics ( ons ) , show there was a net flow of 298,000 migrants to the uk in the year to september last year , equal to the population of a city roughly the size of nottingham and up from 210,000 in the previous 12 months . the increase in net migration was driven by a ' statistically significant ' rise in immigrants arriving in the uk - up to 624,000 in the year to september 2014 from 530,000 in the previous 12 months .
