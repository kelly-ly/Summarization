prehistoric humans living in a cave 14,700 years ago were cannibals and made cups from the skulls of the dead , a new study has found . researchers at the natural history museum in london and university college london have found evidence that the human bones found in gough 's cave in somerset had the flesh cut from them before being chewed and crushed . this skull found at gough 's cave in somerset had been carefully shaped into a bowl around 14,700 years ago .
