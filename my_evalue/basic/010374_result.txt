a british mother is among nearly 100 women who have had babies by a danish sperm donor who has triggered an international scare over the spread of an incurable genetic disease . with demand for danish sperm soaring , the donor is understood to have fathered 99 ‘ viking babies’ – as they are widely dubbed – across the world through the clinic nordic cryobank . but reports from a variety of countries show the donor has fathered many more children – 99 in total , including the ones on belgium , along with 44 across scandinavia , 34 in america and one in iceland .
