hibernian claimed their first edinburgh derby victory of the season as goals from jason cummings and farid el alagui sealed a 2-0 win over hearts and allowed them to collect a valuable three points in the push for second place in the championship . with the title wrapped up , hearts went into this match with only derby bragging rights at stake . on paper , the game meant more to promotion-chasing hibs and alan stubbs ' side fully deserved all the spoils . the hosts then went on a three-against-one counter-attack charge led by dominique malonga but the congo international selfishly hit the ball wide when martin boyle was in vast amounts of space at the edge of the area .
