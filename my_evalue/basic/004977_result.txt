an armenian expat who shows australians how to cook a ' real barbecue ' has become an internet sensation after his daughter posted his hilarious video online . yervand garginian , a 60-year-old bus driver from south croydon in melbourne , filmed himself cooking chicken on an open charcoal barbecue using a traditional ' khorovats ' recipe at a recent family gathering . in the video mr garginian , who moved to australia from armenia in 1977 , dismisses the australian-style barbecue of cooking ' oily ' sausages .
