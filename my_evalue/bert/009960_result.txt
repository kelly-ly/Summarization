many britons have given up on their dream of owning a home amid high house prices and poor pay rises , research has found . it found that just four in 10 renters ( 43 per cent ) aged 20 to 45 are saving for a deposit to buy a home , compared to almost half a year ago ( 49 per cent ) . non-homeowners said they would be willing to save for about five years and four months to save for a deposit .
