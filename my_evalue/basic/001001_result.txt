( cnn ) canadian actor jonathan crombie , who co-starred in the `` anne of green gables '' tv movies , died this week at age 48 . crombie died wednesday from complications of a brain hemorrhage , `` anne of green gables '' producer kevin sullivan said . based on canadian author lucy maud montgomery 's children 's books , `` anne of green gables '' debuted in canada on cbc tv in 1984 and became a cultural touchstone .
