at least 400 migrants trying to reach europe from libya were killed after their boat capsized , it emerged tonight . the stories emerged after more than 8,000 migrants took advantage of calm weather to cross the mediterranean over the past weekend . more than 170,000 migrants arrived in italy last year after being packed on to boats by people smugglers . meanwhile , the eu’s frontex border agency said traffickers trying to recover a boat had fired shots into the air to warn away the coastguard – sparking concerns for the safety of rescue workers and migrants .
