( cnn ) more than 500 houthi rebels have been killed since the start of saudi-led military operations against yemeni shia fighters , a saudi defense ministry official said saturday , according to the state-run saudi press agency . a saudi general said saturday the nine-nation coalition has undertaken 1,200 airstrikes since they began on march 26 . three saudi military officers were killed and two others were wounded in the shelling , a defense official said , according to spa .
