battered , rusted and covered in overgrown weeds , this unremarkable looking bus and train were used to film one of the most memorable hollywood action scenes of the past 25 years . signage identifying the bus as a prison transport for the purposes of the film remains on its front . director andrew davis used 13 cameras to film the scene and three were destroyed in the process . the inside of the bus will be familiar to fans of the film as the location where harrison ford 's character attempts to free fellow prisoners before leaping out of the path of the oncoming train .
