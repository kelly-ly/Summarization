australia hooker nathan charles has spoken out about being born with and overcoming cystic fibrosis to pursue a successful rugby career . charles took to rugby as a youngster as a way of battling with his illness and has since turned professional . in a heartfelt interview , charles sheds light on to the daily struggles he has to overcome , but insists that he is prepared to , because he wants to be defined by his abilities on the field and not by his condition . typically , sufferers from cystic fibrosis are not expected to live beyond the age of 17 , but charles has spent his entire life defying this disease .
