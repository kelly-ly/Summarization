the mass stranding of 160 melon-headed whales on two beaches in japan has triggered fears of upcoming earthquake and tsunami . the whales , which are in fact a species of dolphin , were found washed up along a 6-mile ( 10km ) stretch of the pacific coast of hokato , around 60 miles ( 100km ) north of tokyo . more than 160 melon-headed whales washed onto two beaches at hokota city , north-east of tokyo .
