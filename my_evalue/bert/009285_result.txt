manchester city star have met up with more than 100 local schoolchildren for the unveiling of a 64metre mural depicting ' the football effect ' at the club 's academy stadium . captain vincent kompany helps a young city fan put the finishing touches to the massive mural . among the images featured are scenes of football being played around the world , including night football , street football and disabled leagues , while the club 's city football academy is at the centre of the design .
