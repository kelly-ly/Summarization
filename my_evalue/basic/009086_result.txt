liverpool manager brendan rodgers has written off manchester city’s chances of signing raheem sterling this summer by declaring the anfield club are bigger than their etihad rivals and making it clear they have no intention of becoming their feeder team . ahead of sunday 's fa cup semi-final against aston villa at wembley , rodgers indicated it could take two decades for sheik mansour’s club to reach the status of either liverpool or manchester united . city have been heavily linked with a £50million move for sterling , who has two years left on his contract and is stalling on a new deal .
