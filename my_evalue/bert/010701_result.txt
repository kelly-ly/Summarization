psv eindhoven beat heerenveen 4-1 to win the dutch championship on saturday for the first time in seven seasons . striker luuk de jong scored two headed goals in the opening period , the first inside three minutes as hosts psv eased the nerves around the philips stadion with a fast start . it is a 22nd league championship for psv , who are second only to ajax ( 33 titles ) in the netherlands . luuk de jong scored two headed first half goals to put his side in control at the philips stadion .
