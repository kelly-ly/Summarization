an entire herd of buffalo has been shot dead by law enforcement in upstate new york after they escaped from a farm . three men hired by the farm were cleared to open fire friday afternoon in a stream in woods in the town of coeymans , about 10 miles south of the state capital albany . the owner of the farm from where they escaped say the group managed to swim across the hudson river . a police helicopter was hovering over them to make sure the group did not split .
