nicola adams has no intention of following her former great britain team-mate natasha jonas into retirement as she focuses on becoming a double olympic gold medallist in rio next year . having overcome another lengthy injury lay-off , adams is preparing to return to an english ring for the first time since her london 2012 triumph at the national championships in liverpool next week . adams said : ' i will keep fighting as long as i have the motivation to do so and there is no reason why that should not continue after the rio olympics . ' at the moment becoming a double olympic champion is the only thing on my mind but i have other goals left to achieve including world gold after only taking three silvers so far . ' despite all my injury problems i have never lost my love for the sport and as long as i keep that love of fighting and entertaining the crowd i will still be eager to compete . ' adams will face a rare challenge to her domestic dominance in liverpool with her 51kg category also including lisa whiteside , who won world silver in adams ' absence earlier this year .
