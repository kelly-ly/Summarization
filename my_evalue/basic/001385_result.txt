michael schumacher 's 16-year-old son mick begins his formula 4 career in germany this weekend . schumacher junior is part of the dutch van amersfoort racing team in the 2015 adac formula 4 series after impressing during his karting career , finishing second in the world , european and german kart championships last season . his debut in single-seater racing gets underway at the motorsport arena oschersleben , which holds the first of eight adac formula 4 meetings .
