( cnn ) pope francis will visit cuba on his way to the united states in september , the vatican said wednesday -- a trip that will come months after he helped negotiate a diplomatic thaw between the two countries . the exact timing of the cuba trip was n't immediately released , but the vatican said the pope would stop in cuba before his planned late september stops in washington , new york and philadelphia . `` the pope coming here , maybe he could ... make more positive ways for cubans to go towards religion , more than politics , '' said raul garcia , a cuban-american who returned to the island .
