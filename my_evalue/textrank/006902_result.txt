oskar groening is being tried on 300,000 counts of accessory to murder , related to a period between may and july 1944 when around 425,000 jews from hungary were brought to the auschwitz-birkenau complex in nazi-occupied poland and most immediately gassed to death . ss sergeant oskar groening - known as ' the bookkeeper of auschwitz ' - is on trial charged with complicity in the killing of 300,000 jews at the nazi extermination camp . groening , who worked for an insurance company after the war , has testified as a witness in other nazi trials .
