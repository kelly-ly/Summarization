head injuries have been under greater scrutiny since hugo lloris refused to be substituted following a clash with romelu lukaku in 2013 , with new rules introduced at the start of the current barclays premier league season stating a player suffering a head injury must leave the pitch and only be allowed to return if cleared by a club doctor . the fa have formed a concussion experts panel to help figure out how to make the game safer for players . recently , the jeff astle foundation has been raising awareness of the danger of long-term head injuries from playing football , with former west brom player astle having died in 2002 of a brain trauma caused by heading heavy leather footballs during his career .
