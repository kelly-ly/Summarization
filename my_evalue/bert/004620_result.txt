a pit crew member was hit by a car on sunday during the inaugural indycar grand prix of louisiana . todd phillips , a front-outside tire changer for dayle coyne racing , was injuried when he was struck by the car of francesco dracone , who had come in on lap 25 for tires and fuel . dracone spun while exiting his put box , clipping phillips ' leg . phillips was taken to the infield care center for treatment where he has received stitches for a cut on his leg and has been released .
