anthony trollope , a famous novelist , introduced post boxes to the uk in 1852 after seeing them in france . although famous as a novelist , trollope also made history by introducing post boxes while working for the post office , after seeing them in france . red has remained the standard colour for uk boxes with only a few exceptions , one being blue post boxes for overseas mail . a celebration of history : sue whalley , royal mail 's chief operating officer , said : ' we are delighted to contribute to this year 's bicentenary celebrations of the birth of anthony trollope ' .
