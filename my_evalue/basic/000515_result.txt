( cnn ) a hooded angel with black wings appeared on tuesday near the spot where walter scott was shot and killed by a police officer in north charleston , south carolina , on saturday . when protesters held the winged figure at a wednesday morning rally outside north charleston 's city hall , the artwork was widely photographed . creator phillip hyman grew up in the neighborhood where scott , an unarmed black man , was shot in the back several times by a white police officer on saturday .
