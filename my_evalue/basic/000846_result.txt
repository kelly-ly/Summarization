for riyadh and its regional allies , the saudi military intervention in yemen -- `` operation decisive storm '' -- is the moment the sunni arab nation finally woke up to repel the expansion of shia-iranian influence . for tehran and its regional allies -- including the houthi movement in yemen -- saudi arabia 's actions are in defense of a retrogressive status quo order that is no longer tenable . unlike in syria and in iraq , the saudi military is now directly battling it out with iranian-backed rebels in yemen .
