an air force veteran and former playboy model was arrested for taking an american flag from campus protesters who were trampling on it . michelle manhart , 38 , was handcuffed by police at valdosta state university , georgia , and driven off in a patrol car after grabbing the stars and stripes and refusing to return it to the student demonstrators . she later appeared in other editions of playboy - including the cover of the mexican edition - and posed for animal rights group peta to support an anti-fur campaign .
