a chinese hospital is being painstakingly moved just eight metres a day on 1,000 metal rollers to prevent it from being demolished to make way for a new road . the building , which was built in the 1960s and belongs to zheng gong hospital in henan province , was under threat of demolition as it is situated in the path of a road expansion project , reports people 's daily online . now , the two-storey hospital outpatient building in the city of zhengzhou , in central china , is being slowly and carefully pushed just a few metres a day on the giant cylinder-shaped rollers .
