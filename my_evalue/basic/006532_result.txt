archaeologists have discovered a previously unknown stretch of the great wall of china in the northwestern of the country , thought to date back more than 2,000 years . nine sections of the wall have been found over the last two months along the inner coast of the yellow river in gansu province and the ningxia region , reported the people 's daily online . historical documents had suggested that the emperor qin ordered for the great wall to start in gansu province and run east into the ningxia region and inner mongolia , but physical evidence in this particular area has never been found until now .
