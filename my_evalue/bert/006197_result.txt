isis was removed from the official list of future pacific hurricane names by the un 's world meteorological organization , a wmo spokeswoman announced on friday . as the name of an ancient goddess of egypt , isis had been on the wmo list of names for 2016 . the hurricane committee also backed mexico 's request to retire the name odile because of the storm ( above ) . the group stands accused of committing brutal atrocities against civilians by un war crimes investigators .
