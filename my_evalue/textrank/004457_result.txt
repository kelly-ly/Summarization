a 73-year-old woman died in front of horrified family members after she was knocked over by a canadian tourist while visiting the great wall of china . an official with the mutianyu great wall tourism services company told news website china.org.cn that the collision occurred in a ‘ blind spot’ within the cctv system , so there is no footage of it . the official said : ‘ we have rules and warnings for tourists , asking them not to chase each other , run fast or play around on the steep sections of the great wall . ’
