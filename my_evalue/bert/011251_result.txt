comedian jenny eclair travelled with her other half on a painting in venus break with flavours . for seven days , we painters were patiently guided by hugh through the basics of sketching , paint technique , colour and form , while the cooks stayed in the kitchen making our meals . i know who picked the short straw . like schoolchildren with credit cards , we roamed the most beautiful city in the world and came home , inspired by its mind-boggling loveliness , with lots of photos to copy into our art books back at our base , villa bianchi .
