the details of where and how the west-sussex born model and mistress of rossetti died remained a mystery for years until her biographer recently tracked her down through hospital records . born sarah cox in 1835 , she met rossetti in 1958 and was considered one of his ' stunners ' - a reference to the women he kept in his company as lovers and models . rossetti was the son of italian scholar gabriele pasquale giuseppe rossetti and his wife frances polidori .
