( cnn ) blue bell ice cream has temporarily shut down one of its manufacturing plants over the discovery of listeria contamination in a serving of ice cream originating from that plant . listeria monocytogenes was recently found in a cup of ice cream recovered from the hospital . in early march , in light of the kansas listeria outbreak , blue bell recalled a group of products made at a plant in texas . five people were infected and three died in the past year in kansas from listeria that might be linked to blue bell creameries products , according to the cdc .
