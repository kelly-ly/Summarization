( cnn ) wikileaks founder julian assange has agreed to be interviewed by swedish prosecutors in london , his lawyer in sweden told cnn . assange has been holed up in the ecuadorian embassy in london since june 2012 to avoid extradition to sweden , where prosecutors want to question him about 2010 allegations that he raped one woman and sexually molested another . according to the lawyer , thomas olsson , swedish prosecutors will now have to reach out to british and ecuadorian authorities to request permission to conduct the interview at the embassy .
