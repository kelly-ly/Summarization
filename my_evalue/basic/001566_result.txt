the st ivan rilski church is the only remaining evidence of the community of zapalnya , bulgaria , where residents lived until they were forced to leave their homes to make way for a dam built by the communist regime . named after the patron saint of bulgaria , the crumbling stone structure now cuts a ghostly figure in a few feet of water at the zhrebchevo reservoir , near the town of tvardica . the partially-submerged ruins of the st ivan rilski church are visible at the zhrebchevo reservoir , near the town of tvardica , bulgaria .
