rome ( cnn ) muslims who were among migrants trying to get from libya to italy in a boat this week threw 12 fellow passengers overboard -- killing them -- because the 12 were christians , italian police said thursday . sometime during the trip north across the mediterranean sea , the alleged assailants -- muslims from the ivory coast , mali and senegal -- threw the 12 overboard , police said . since 2000 , according to the international organization for migration , almost 22,000 people have died fleeing across the mediterranean . cnn 's ben wedeman contributed to this report .
