( cnn ) sarah brady , who with her husband , james brady , pushed for stricter gun control laws , including the brady handgun violence prevention act , died friday , her family said . `` sarah courageously stepped up after jim was shot to prevent others from enduring what our family has gone through , and her work has saved countless lives , '' their statement said . she was the chairwoman of the brady campaign to prevent gun violence and the brady center to prevent gun violence from 2000 until she died .
