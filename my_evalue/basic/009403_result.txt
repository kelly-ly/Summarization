alinea , run by the talented grant achatz has retained its crown as the best restaurant in the world , the annual poll by luxury magazine elite traveler revealed . three restaurants in south africa - the test kitchen , cape town ( 44 ) ; tasting room at le quartier francais , franschhoek ( 85 ) , and rust en vrede , stellenbosch ( 87 ) - were considered the best in the country . the awards also awarded its chef of the year accolade , which this year went to swiss chef daniel humm , chef and co-owner of eleven madison park , which came in a fourth in the list and the nomad in new york city , which placed 89th .
