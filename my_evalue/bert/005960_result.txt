when something goes bump in the night , the last thing you expect is for grandad 's old cane to be responsible . from the indiana ' ghost cane ' to the ' chair of death ' , these regular household fixtures are sure to give you the creeps . during the middle of the 19th century in south carolina , jacob cooley murdered a slave named hosea for building a chest which did not meet his standards . when mary anderson’s grandson saw the ghost of his grandfather in the family home in indiana , she decided to ease the boy’s fears by selling the cane he had owned .
