( cnn ) a prosecutor has dismissed allegations that argentine president cristina fernandez de kirchner tried to cover up iran 's involvement in a 1994 bombing in buenos aires . the move by prosecutor javier de luca to drop the case could mean a definitive end to the accusations that have roiled the nation , according to argentina 's state-run telam news agency . on monday , de luca announced that in his investigation he found that `` there was no crime here , either carried out or attempted , '' according to telam .
