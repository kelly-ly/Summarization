konstantin sivkov , who writes a column for the military-industrial courier , wrote earlier this month that russia needed new ' megaweapons ' to unleash chaos and guarantee destruction of its enemies . in an article titled ' nuclear special forces ' he said that russia should develop nuclear weapons manned by a small force that can cause tsunamis on the us coasts and force a volcano in yellowstone national park to erupt . he said that the main objective of the new system would serve as an asymmetric threat against the us , which is ' moving to the borders or russia ' .
