barbie collectors mark your calendars : this saturday , the very first barbie doll will go on sale again in australia . the first 100 customers at each of the melbourne , sydney , perth , brisbane , and adelaide city stores on saturday april 11 at 9am can snap up the collectors ' items for the special heritage price . iconic : the dolls are newly produced but modelled on the original barbie doll that was first released at the new york toy fair in 1959 . fifties fashion : the doll features a black and white striped one-piece swimsuit , black heels , gold hoop earrings and white sunglasses .
