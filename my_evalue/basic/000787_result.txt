washington ( cnn ) this week is the 100th anniversary of what many historians acknowledge as the armenian genocide -- the turkish massacre of an estimated 1.5 million armenians . and it 's also the seventh year in a row president barack obama has broken his promise to use the word `` genocide '' to describe the atrocity . on the 2008 campaign trail , obama promised to use the word `` genocide '' to describe the 1915 massacre by turks of armenians -- a pledge he made when seeking armenian-american votes .
