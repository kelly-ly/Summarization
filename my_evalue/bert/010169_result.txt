a spanish town on the camino de santiago pilgrimage route is warning pilgrims and other visitors to stop defecating along the famous path . in lastres , angry residents have complained about pilgrims defecating outside their homes . the pilgrimage begins at various points , with the longest route nearly 500 miles from the spain-france border , which lead to the cathedral of santiago de compostela , where saint james the great is said to be buried . julio gallego told the spanish newspaper la voz de galicia that it is a ‘ huge failure’ that needs to be remedied .
