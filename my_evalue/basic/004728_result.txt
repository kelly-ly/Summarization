sam bennett has revealed the extent of the shocking injuries he sustained after the bora-argon 18 cyclist was involved in 50-rider pile-up in the final kilometre of the scheldeprijs one day classic . the 24-year was taken to hospital after the crash in belgium on monday and , although he escaped without any long-lasting injury , bennett suffered severe cuts on his back and shoulders . team sky riders bradley wiggins and geraint thomas were both involved in the race but managed to avoid the crash , but team-mate elia viviani collided with a barrier and was taken to hospital for x-rays complaining of pain in his arm .
