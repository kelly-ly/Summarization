in the first series , the historic city of mdina , malta , was used as the filming location , before switching to croatia at the start of series two , a move which allowed creators to film near the sea . when the show runners switched to filming in croatia at the start of series two , they were able to film near the adriatic sea . island officials took issue with the crew over potential damage inflicted on the natural wonder and thus , producers were forced to change filming locations at the start of series three .
