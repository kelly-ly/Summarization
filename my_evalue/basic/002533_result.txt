charles and camilla finally married following a relationship spanning 35 years in a relatively quiet civil ceremony at the windsor guildhall on the 9th april 2005 . since then , the duchess of cornwall as she is now known , has become an integral part of the royal family - and would be cheerfully accepted as charles ' queen by 50 per cent of britons . that marks a significant turnaround from the duchess ' earliest days as a royal , with camilla following charles ' lead and often appearing nervous in front of photographers .
