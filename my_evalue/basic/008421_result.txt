former leeds and england coach tony smith admits he was shocked by kevin sinfield 's decision to switch codes but , on reflection , believes it could be an inspired decision . sinfield , 34 , the greatest captain in leeds ' history , stunned the world of rugby league with his sudden announcement that he will quit the sport at the end of the season and join the rhinos ' sister club yorkshire carnegie on an 18-month contract . sinfield , who is closing in on third place in rugby league 's all-time scoring list with 3,997 points , told a news conference at headingley he did not want to play against rhinos and that the opportunity to spearhead carnegie 's bid for a premiership return was too good to resist .
