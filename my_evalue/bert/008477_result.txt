an australian businesswoman has come out on top against the world 's 13th richest billionaire after stopping benefit cosmetics from using the term ' brow bar ' exclusively . beautician chernae noonan , who founded eyebrow shaping business the brow bar , has blocked the cosmetic giant 's application to trademark the phrase . ms noonan started her business in 2003 in her springwood home and since then it has seen ' significant growth ' . hearing officer nicole worth said she did not consider the word ' brow bar ' to be used commonly enough to be deemed as ' generic ' as lawyers for benefit argued .
