debt : parents are getting into debt by letting their adult children live at home for longer . now , debt organisations have warned that parents should not be afraid to ask their children for rent and money towards household bills amid fears the british ‘ stiff upper lip’ makes them reluctant to admit when they need help . according to debt management specialist payplan , a third of parents in debt who have grown-up children living at home have been forced to take out a loan to meet the costs of housing and feeding them .
