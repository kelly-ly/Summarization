an israeli geologist claims he has ‘confirmed’ the existence and authenticity of a tomb belonging to jesus and his son in jerusalem . the research could have enormous ramifications as it suggests that jesus was married , fathered a child and that a physical resurrection did not take place . jacobovici told the jerusalem post : ‘ this find illustrates that the james ossuary is authentic and the jesus family tomb indeed belongs to the family of jesus of nazareth . ’ dr shumron told the new york times : ‘ i think i’ve got really powerful , virtually unequivocal evidence that the james ossuary spent most of its lifetime , or death time , in the talpiot tomb . ’
