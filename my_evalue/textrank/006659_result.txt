in march , an independent investigation into what happened at furness general hospital between 2004 and 2013 , found failures led to the avoidable deaths of 11 babies and one mother . feuding medics , an obsession with natural childbirth , and a tight knit group of midwives who dubbed themselves ' the musketeers ' contributed to a ' distressing chain of events ' which led to avoidable harm to mothers and babies , at furness general hospital in barrow , cumbria . the hearing heard that ms ratcliffe qualified as a midwife in 1990 and in 1997 she was promoted to a band 7 midwife at furness general hospital , part of the university hospitals of morecambe bay nhs foundation trust , where she worked until january 2014 .
