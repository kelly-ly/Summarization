nasa has released the first close-up views of the north pole of dwarf planet ceres from the dawn spacecraft . dawn arrived on 6 march , the first time a spacecraft has orbited a dwarf planet , and is now preparing for the key science phase of the mission . the latest images were taken on 10 april from a distance of 21,000 miles ( 33,000km ) , and are the highest-resolution views of ceres so far .
