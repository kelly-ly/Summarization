anti-semitic attacks surged worldwide by 38 per cent last year , with the highest number of incidents occurring in france , an annual study has revealed . anger in western europe over israel 's military offensive in the gaza strip last july and august , as well as anti-jewish caricatures in the media and social networks had helped to turn anti-semitism into an ' almost daily phenomena seen and felt in every sphere of life ' , the report said . britain registered 141 violent incidents against jews in 2014 , after 95 a year earlier . the violence began when islamist militants carried out a massacre in the office of charlie hebdo magazine .
