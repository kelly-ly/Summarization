the owner of a british seaside hotel says it is virus-free after 100 holidaymakers were struck down by what health officials have described as a norovirus outbreak . dozens of people who visited the cooden beach hotel earlier this month came down with vomiting , diarrhoea and other symptoms of the highly-contagious airborne bug . a rother district council spokesperson said it is believed that 100 people have been affected by the so-called ' winter vomiting bug ' after visiting the hotel in bexhill-on-sea , east sussex .
