the continents of north and south america came together much earlier than previously thought , according to researchers who found evidence in rock deposits from ancient rivers in colombia of the land bridge that connected the long-isolated landmasses . the two continents are linked at panama , but there has been a debate about when this land bridge first appeared , with most experts placing its formation at about 3 million years ago . the various continents , sitting atop huge plates akin to jigsaw puzzle pieces sliding over earth 's mantle , have slowly moved around the globe during the planet 's history .
