a tourist has a frightening tale to tell after french customs officers found a stash of cocaine in a suitcase that had his name on it but didn’t belong to him . the department said their investigation found that smugglers in the dominican republic filled the suitcase with cocaine and used the tourist’s identity in an attempt to sneak the drugs into france through nice cote d’azur international airport . last year french customs officers seized more than six tonnes of cocaine at the country’s ports of entry .
