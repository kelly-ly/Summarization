a classic aston martin once owned by spartacus star peter ustinov is set to fetch more than £1million at auction - twice what it fetched four years ago . it was one of the most luxurious cars of its day , costing £4,000 and was delivered to him at a swiss hotel at a time when the average house price in britain was just £2,500 . i am spartacus : star kirk douglas , centre right , plays a gladiator who takes on the roman empire in the 1960 stanley kubrick film . it was launched to great fanfare and praise in 1958 at the london motor show .
