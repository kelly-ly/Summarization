amazon is trialling a scheme that could spell the end of waiting in for deliveries or collecting your parcels from a local locker . select prime customers will soon be given the option to have their goods delivered to the boot , or trunk , of their car without needing to be near the vehicle . the pilot scheme is launching in munich next month and amazon has teamed up with audi and dhl to run the initiative . once ordered , a dhl delivery driver will receive a temporary digital access code .
