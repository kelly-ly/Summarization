( the hollywood reporter ) the original cast of twin peaks is backing david lynch in his salary standoff with showtime . the stars have teamed together for a video backing the show 's co-creator with a # savetwinpeaks campaign that says doing the revival without lynch is `` like pies without cherries , '' among other nods to the original drama series . showtime already had a deal in place with lynch and co-creator mark frost to bring back the cult hit with star kyle maclachlan for a run in 2016 , with sources telling thr that the scripts had already been written .
