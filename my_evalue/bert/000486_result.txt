( cnn ) eight iranian border guards have been killed in clashes with militants near the border with pakistan , iranian state media reported . three of the militants were killed by iranian forces in the fighting monday in the southeastern town of negur , the state-run news agency irna reported . jaish al adal has also claimed responsibility for attacks on iranian territory . if the militants entered pakistan after the attack , `` they will be apprehended and brought to justice , '' it said .
