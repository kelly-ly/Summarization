derry mathews will take on venezuelan knockout artist ismael barroso for the interim wba world title after richar abril pulled out of their april 18 date . abril was initially forced to withdraw from his title defence against mathews in march after coming down with dengue fever from a mosquito bite and he withdrew again this week as he is still reeling from the after-effects of the virus that has left him unable to train . derry was set to fight cuban wba champion richar abril but will instead face barroso on the april 18 date .
