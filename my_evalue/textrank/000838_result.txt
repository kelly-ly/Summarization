( cnn ) jodi arias was sentenced to life in prison monday for the gruesome 2008 murder of her ex-boyfriend travis alexander . maricopa county judge sherry stephens could have sentenced arias to life with the possibility of early release after 25 years , but decided the convicted killer should spend the rest of her life behind bars . arias will serve her sentence at the lumley unit in the arizona state prison complex-perryville near goodyear , arizona .
