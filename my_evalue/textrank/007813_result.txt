the world’s largest aerospace company is showing off its roomier overhead luggage bins , which will be available on its 737 family of planes . designed for future aircraft which will be used by the likes of delta air lines and alaska airlines , the ‘ space bins’ can hold 50 per cent more carry-on luggage than existing compartments on the current fleet of 737s . finding a spot for a carry-on bag , especially during a budget flight that charges for checked bags , can be one of the most stressful experiences for passengers . the chicago-based manufacturer said the ‘ space bins’ have a lower lip height , making it easier to load bags and see inside .
