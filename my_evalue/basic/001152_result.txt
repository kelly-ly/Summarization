world snooker chairman barry hearn has ruled out awarding a season-long tour wild card to reanne evans . the 10-time winner of the ladies ' world championship saw her crucible dream ended by ken doherty after a tight clash on thursday . doherty , the 1997 world champion , led the calls for evans to be handed another chance on the main tour , but hearn says the 29-year-old from dudley must go through the qualifying school to earn a place .
