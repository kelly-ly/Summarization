an airline passenger who was stabbed with a pen by a woman sitting next to him because he was snoring compared the bizarre attack to being ' stung by bees ' . lenny mordarski , 68 , was viciously poked with the blue ballpoint on a flight to new hampshire on thursday because he fell asleep and was snoring before take-off . the woman , dubbed ' psycho pen lady ' by another fellow passenger , was removed from the flight . southwest airlines supplied the men with free gin and tonics and when the plane landed , a flight attendant said : ' please keep your writing utensils to yourself and have a great day ' .
