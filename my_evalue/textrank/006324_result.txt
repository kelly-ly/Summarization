derry mathews will take on venezuelan knockout artist ismael barroso for the interim wba world title after richar abril pulled out of their april 18 date . derry was set to fight cuban wba champion richar abril but will instead face barroso on the april 18 date . mathews expressed his disappointment at losing out on a title shot with abril , who was twice forced to withdraw through illness . southpaw barroso , a frightening puncher from el tigre , has recorded 16 knockouts from 17 wins in his 19 fights ( two draws ) , with 11 quick wins coming inside the first three rounds .
