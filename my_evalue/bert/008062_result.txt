while easter in the uk is celebrated with chocolate eggs and a roast lamb , in greece they mark the day slightly differently - and a week later . although small firework displays are held at churches around the country at midnight services on easter saturday - despite official disapproval from church officials - vrontados is in a league of its own , drawing spectators from all over greece and abroad . russian president vladimir putin attended the easter sunday service in moscow , meeting with the patriach of moscow and all russia kirill , while pope francis held a commemorative service in st peter 's basilica in the vatican with armenian clergy to mark 100 years since the armenian genocide . a woman clutches a candle and what looks to be an incense stick as others queue at the cathedral of the transfiguration of jesus .
