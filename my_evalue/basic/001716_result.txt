the biggest fight in boxing history has sold out within a minute of going on sale with tickets for floyd mayweather vs manny pacquiao at the mgm grand in las vegas on may 2 being snapped up on thursday evening . just minutes after 8pm uk time ( 12 noon in las vegas ) , tickets for the richest bout in history began appearing on the secondary market with one ticket going for a cool $ 128,705 ( £85,508 ) on stubhub . after weeks of wrangling and accusations between mayweather promotions and pacquiao’s promoter bob arum , a batch of between 500 and 1,000 tickets were made available to boxing fans for the mega-fight in may .
