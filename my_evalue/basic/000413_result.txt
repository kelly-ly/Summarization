kathmandu , nepal ( cnn ) the death toll from the devastating earthquake that struck nepal two days ago surged past 3,000 on monday , a government official said . the number of people confirmed dead in nepal stands at 3,218 , said nepalese ministry of home affairs spokesman laxmi dhakal . stunned residents wandered the streets of kathmandu , the capital city of roughly 3 million people that 's now the focus of international disaster relief efforts .
