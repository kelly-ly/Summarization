( cnn ) wikileaks founder julian assange has agreed to be interviewed by swedish prosecutors in london , his lawyer in sweden told cnn . `` my view has always been that to perform an interview with him at the ecuadorian embassy in london would lower the quality of the interview , and that he would need to be present in sweden in any case should there be a trial in the future , '' ny said . the australian national has not been charged and denies the claims . ecuador granted assange political asylum in 2012 .
