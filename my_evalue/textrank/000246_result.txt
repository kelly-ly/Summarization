( cnn ) the quaint town of dunblane , scotland , has been set abuzz by the wedding of tennis legend andy murray to his long-term girlfriend , kim sears . saturday 's event -- dubbed `` the royal wedding of scotland '' -- took place at dunblane cathedral , with cheering crowds spilling onto the streets to support their home-grown talent . the grand slam and olympic champion donned a traditional blue and green tartan kilt , while his bride dazzled in a vintage-style gown by british designer jenny packham .
