an enormous diorama which shows the full extent of the battle of waterloo has gone on display to mark the 200th anniversary of the conflict . the model contains more than 30,000 figures , and illustrates all the key moments of the battle which allowed the duke of wellington to defeat napoleon 's forces . model : this diorama shows the full extent of the battle of waterloo , which took place almost exactly 200 years ago . despite the enormous scale of the diorama , the number of troops featured is just a third of the true number who were killed or wound in the battle - and less than 10 per cent of the total number who took part .
