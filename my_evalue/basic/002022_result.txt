english referee mark clattenburg will take charge of the champions league quarter-final clash between paris saint-germain and barcelona on wednesday night , and he will be assisted by five other englishmen . completing the all-english officials team at the parc des princes on wednesday will be assistants simon beck and jake collin , additional assistants anthony taylor and andre marriner and fourth official stuart burt . clattenburg took charge of the manchester derby at the weekend and elected not to send off manchester city captain vincent kompany for a rash challenge on daley blind in the first half that many observers saw as deserving of a red card .
