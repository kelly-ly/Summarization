the nepal earthquake that has killed at least 2,500 people was a ' nightmare waiting to happen ' , disaster experts said last night . the united states geological survey said the quake struck 81 kilometres ( 50 miles ) northwest of kathmandu at 06.11 gmt , with walls crumbling and families racing outside of their homes . a 7.8 magnitude earthquake could kill 10 to 30 per one million residents in california , but has killed at least 1,900 in nepal , and could kill up to 10,000 in parts of pakistan , india , iran and china , said u.s. geological survey seismologist david wald .
