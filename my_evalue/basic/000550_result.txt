( cnn ) countries around the world have launched massive aid operations to help victims of the nepal earthquake , but distribution of aid faces challenges as nepal is still trying to come to terms with the scale of the disaster . with over 4,400 dead , 8,000 injured and 8 million people across nepal affected , numerous aid groups and at least 16 nations have rushed to send supplies and workers to the stricken country . but now the nepalese government and army who are leading the disaster response face another problem : how to effectively coordinate and organize the massive influx of humanitarian aid .
