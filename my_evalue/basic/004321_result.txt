on friday , ruben costa , 35 , pleaded guilty to impersonating a member of the police force , but was only handed a two-month suspended sentence , according to the nt news . the incident occurred when the northern territory man returned home from work to find his wife had left him , taking their son and possessions . prosecutor marilyn lester told the darwin magistrates court that costa told the taxi operator that he was searching for a woman who had abducted a young boy .
