armenian groups and many scholars say that turks planned and carried out genocide , starting in 1915 , when more than a million ethnic armenians were massacred in the final years of the ottoman empire . turkey officially denies that a genocide took place , saying hundreds of thousands of armenian christians and turkish muslims died in intercommunal violence around the bloody battlefields of world war i . the armenian government and influential armenian diaspora groups have urged countries around the world to formally label the 1915 events as genocide .
