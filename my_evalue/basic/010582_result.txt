a water main break in new york city caused evening commute hysteria wednesday as a surge of brown water poured into a west village subway tunnel sending travelers scrambling for cover and a different route home . the 12-inch water main broke around 6:45pm at 13th street and seventh avenue in manhattan , reports cbs new york , shutting down three train lines in both directions . the official new york city transit subway twitter account posted pictures starting around 9pm showing crews working toward getting the the water pumped out .
