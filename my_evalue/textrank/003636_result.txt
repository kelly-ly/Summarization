the over the counter drug phenergan is commonly used to treat allergies however it contains a sedative called promethazine which is widely known to cause breathing problems in young children . the medical community claim the sedative phenergan should not be used on children younger than two years old as side effects like respiratory depression , more commonly known as hypoventilation , can be fatal . warnings about the use of drugs like phenergan on children have been around since 2006 when the us food and drug administration found the medication was linked to 22 cases of respiratory depression in infants , seven of which were fatal .
