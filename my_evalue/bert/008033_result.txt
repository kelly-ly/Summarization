technology may have advanced in leaps and bounds , but when it comes to our most precious papers , it turns out many of us remain creatures of habit . a third of adults shun the storage possibilities of the internet and still keep their most important papers in a shoebox , the poll by online file-sharing service lifetile found . an ultra cautious 4 per cent have safety deposit boxes , said the survey . lifetile is a service offering an online site to keep private documents , photos and other memories to build up a family history to share with children and others online .
