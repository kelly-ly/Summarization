a real-life willy wonka has laid claim to making the most extravagant chocolate creation - a seven-foot-tall , five-foot-wide easter egg made using 352lbs ( 160kg ) of chocolate . jan hansen 's egg is so big that it took him three days to put together and is made from layers of belgian chocolate which covers an iron frame . the 58-year-old chocolatier commissioned an iron worker to make a special frame for his egg , which was then wrapped in grease proof paper and cling film , before being covered in chocolate .
