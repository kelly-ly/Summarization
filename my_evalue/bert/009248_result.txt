dagenham and redbridge midfielder joss labadie has been banned for six months after being found guilty of a biting offence for the second time in the space of a year . the 24-year-old was last month charged with violent conduct by the fa after he was accused of sinking his teeth into the hand of stevenage defender ronnie henry during dagenham 's 1-0 win at the lamex stadium . labadie appeared at an fa disciplinary hearing on wednesday and denied any wrongdoing , but was hit by another lengthy ban . the incident between labadie and henry occurred during a league two clash last month .
