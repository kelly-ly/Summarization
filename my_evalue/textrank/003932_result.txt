the oxford university women 's boat race team were rescued from the thames by the royal national lifeboat institution ( rnli ) on wednesday after being overcome by choppy waters . crew members from the chiswick rnli station came to the assistance of the oxford crew and their cox , who were training for the boat race which - along with the men 's race - takes place on saturday , april 11 . the rnli alternative boat race fundraising event on april 10 takes place the day before the bny mellon boat race on the same famous stretch of river .
