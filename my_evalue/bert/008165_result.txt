for most people a hangover will have them reaching for the painkillers and greasy breakfast , but those living in ancient egypt had a rather different approach . ancient egyptians would did not have modern hangover cures and used strings of leaves instead , according to new translations of 1,900 year old texts found at oxyrhynchus in egypt . the source of the papyrus is unknown , but it was said to have been found between the legs of a mummy in the theban necropolis . another treatment , this time for intestinal worms , required the physician to cook the cores of dates and a desert plant called colocynth together in sweet beer .
