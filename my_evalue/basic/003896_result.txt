two men have been arrested after a thomson airways flight from london to mexico was forced to divert to bermuda . the boeing 787-8 departed from london gatwick at around 9.40am yesterday , and was seven-and-a-half hours into its flight to cancun when it landed at lf wade international airport in bermuda . a statement on the bermuda police website read : ' around 12:55pm on sunday , april 12 police officers responded to a report of two disruptive passengers on a diverted thomson airways flight landing at lf wade international airport .
