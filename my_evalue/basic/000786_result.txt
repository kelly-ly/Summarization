( cnn ) a former u.s. navy aircraft carrier that survived a japanese torpedo strike and was a massive guinea pig for two atomic bomb blasts looks remarkably intact at the bottom of the pacific , according to federal researchers who surveyed the wreck last month with an underwater drone . `` after 64 years on the seafloor , independence sits on the bottom as if ready to launch its planes , '' mission leader james delgado , the maritime heritage director for noaa 's office of national marine sanctuaries , said in a statement . the ship returned to california for repairs and made it back across the pacific by july 1944 to participate in the battle of the sibuyan sea and the sinking of one of the japanese imperial navy 's biggest warships , the battleship musashi .
