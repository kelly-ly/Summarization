it 's the hallowed turf where the modern day game of rugby was born when one of its pupils picked up a football and ran with it in the early 19th century . rugby school is known as the spiritual home of the game , with the world cup trophy named the webb ellis cup in honour of the schoolboy called william who made the daring decision to disregard the rules in 1823 . order among the chaos : the first handwritten rules of the game of rugby , dated august 28 , 1845 , are viewable at the school 's museum . rich tradition : pupils at the school walk to lessons .
