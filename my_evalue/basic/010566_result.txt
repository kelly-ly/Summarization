a former high school teacher in south carolina has pleaded guilty to having sexual relations with two of her male students . former english teacher kimberly waddell macemore , 25 , of of wilkesboro , south carolina , pleaded guilty on tuesday to having sexual relations with two 17-year-old boys she was supposed to be teaching . macemore was a second year english teacher at west wilkes high school when she was suspended without pay following her arrest in may 2014 .
