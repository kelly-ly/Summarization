england batsman ian bell has signed a three-year contract extension with warwickshire that will keep him at the club until the end of the 2017 season . the 32-year-old has gone on to play 105 test matches and 161 one-day internationals for england . bell has won five trophies with warwickshire , including the the county championship in 2004 and 2012 , the b & h cup in 2002 , the clydesdale bank 40 in 2010 and most recently the natwest t20 blast at edgbaston last season .
