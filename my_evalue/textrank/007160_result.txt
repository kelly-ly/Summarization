british guantanamo bay detainee shaker aamer ( pictured ) is expected to be freed in june , us government sources have revealed . mr aamer , who lived in south london with his wife and children , is the longest-serving uk resident in guantanamo and has twice been cleared for release , by the bush administration in 2007 , and by barack obama in 2009 . mr aamer was sent to guantanamo bay in february 2002 after being detained in kabul , afghanistan while , his representatives claim , he was carrying out peaceful voluntary work for an islamic charity .
