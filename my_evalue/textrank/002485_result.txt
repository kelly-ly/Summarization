for a limited time transavia france is offering cheap flights with packets of crisps , gummy bears and cereal bars at participating shops . each package contains a voucher that can be redeemed for a discounted flight to one of three destinations . billed as ‘ the first snacks with airline tickets’ promotion , the products are being sold at participating carrefour city shops , in selecta vending machines at two paris metro stations , and at an mk2 cinema in paris . passengers can already fly to dublin from paris orly airport from €40 , even without a voucher code .
