an eccentric man is fighting to keep his driving licence photo as a picture of him with a colander on his head because it is his ' religious right ' . the father-of-one has already been turned down once by the government agency but has sent off a fresh photo wearing the headgear in a second bid . mr harris began following the religion , along with his daughter astri , 4 , after it was set up in the usa in 2005 to protest the teaching of creationism as an alternative to evolution in science classes . mr harris , who plays banjo in the iron boot scrapers , vows to fight his corner until he runs out of appeals when he will be forced to send a photo of him without the colander .
