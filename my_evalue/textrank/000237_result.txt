tokyo electric power company ( tepco ) deployed the remote-controlled robot on friday inside one of the damaged reactors that had suffered a meltdown following a devastating earthquake and tsunami in 2011 . the robot , set out to collect data on radiation levels and investigate the spread of debris , stalled after moving about 10 meters , according to a statement released by tepco . it managed to collect data on radiation levels in 14 of the 18 targeted locations .
