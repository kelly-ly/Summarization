a mexican cartel boss known as ' the liquidator ' has been detained by the country 's authorities , who claim he is ' linked to the bloodiest events ever recorded in juarez city ' . mexican federal authorities arrested juarez cartel boss jesus salas aguayo on friday , bringing an end to the career of a criminal who was famed for dispatching his enemies with the use of dynamite . mexican authorities link over twenty deaths directly to the liquidator , including the 2009 murder of a state witness in el paso , a 2010 car-bombing which claimed the lives of two federal police officers and a 2012 massacre of fifteen people in a juarez city bar .
