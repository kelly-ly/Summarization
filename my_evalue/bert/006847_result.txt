the mysterious moai statues of easter island have gazed inland for hundreds of years . a tall order : physicists believe that the pukao , or ' hats , ' were rolled up ramps to reach the top of the figures ( examples are pictured left and right ) , some of which measure up to 40 ft ( 12 metres ) tall . some experts believe the stones were walked into place , while others have suggested that locals chopped down trees to roll the statues into position , unwittingly destroying the forests and causing their own demise through lack of natural resources . but in 2013 , scientists claimed they may have been walked into position .
