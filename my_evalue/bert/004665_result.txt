a students’ union has been accused of racism and sexism after banning white people and men from an event to promote equality . the event , held on wednesday , was organised by welfare and diversity officer bahar mustafa , who said she hoped to persuade academics to broaden courses to include more material relating to minority groups . the ban on men and white students is surprising , since the organisers claim to promote ‘ zero tolerance’ for ‘ all forms of prejudice , discrimination and oppression’ . miss mustafa recently graduated from goldsmiths with an ma in gender and media studies .
