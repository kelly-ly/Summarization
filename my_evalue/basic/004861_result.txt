patrick bamford enhanced middlesbrough’s automatic promotion bid by equalling the club’s record goal return of the modern era . his deflected 20th-minute effort was enough to earn victory over relegation-threatened wigan , who now have just six matches to avert the prospect of playing league one football just two years after shocking manchester city in the fa cup final at wembley . bamford celebrates his 17th goal of the season , the fourth that he has scored in his last four games .
