the time has come for australia’s favourite rock star-turned-politician peter garrett to sell his charming victorian terrace in randwick in sydney’s affluent eastern suburbs . as to be expected the 193 centimetre rockstar’s home boasts beautiful high ceilings . the family of five also own a property in mittagong in nsw’s south coast , ensuring they can enjoy the best of both city and country dwelling . the winding staircase leads up from the lounge room to the bedrooms .
