this week’s warm weather may have tempted you to take a dip in the uk’s usually chilly waters . and if you had , you wouldn’t have been the only one swimming around the coastline . billions of jellyfish have appeared in our waters , apparently attracted by the higher sea temperatures . matt slater of the cornwall wildlife trust said it was ‘ difficult to say what is causing their appearance , but it could be because there is more plankton for the jellyfish to feed on because of warmer waters’ .
