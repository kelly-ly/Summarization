the remaining family members allegedly caught trying to sneak into syria with the son of a labour councillor have been arrested after being brought back to the uk from turkey . they were among eight relatives stopped at the turkish border with syria on april 1 with university student waheed ahmed , the 21-year-old son of labour councillor shakil ahmed . the four children on the trip aged one , three , eight and 11 , have also been flown back to the uk . late on monday , a 30-year-old man from rochdale – where ahmed and the other eight detatined in turkey all live – was arrested on suspicion of the same offence .
