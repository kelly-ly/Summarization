( cnn ) blue bell ice cream has temporarily shut down one of its manufacturing plants over the discovery of listeria contamination in a serving of ice cream originating from that plant . it is the third time blue bell has taken action in light of a listeria outbreak at a kansas hospital that served the company 's ice cream . in a separate outbreak in texas , the cdc did find that listeria samples taken from patients who came down with listeriosis between 2010 and 2014 in a hospital that served 3-ounce blue bell cups matched the listeria in recovered samples .
