england suffered an injury scare at the start of their practice ' match ' here in st kitts when stuart broad dramatically slipped in his delivery stride and was left clutching his ankle on the floor . broad had complained about the state of the crease , which was damp after overnight rain , after bowling just three balls of his first over of the day at warner park . the groundsmen placed sand on the crease but from the very next delivery broad slipped and went crashing to the ground , a blow which necessitated lengthy treatment before he left the field .
