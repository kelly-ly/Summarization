an american cable and satellite television network has pledged to crackdown on australians who use overseas accounts to access the us-restricted streaming service hbo now . tens of thousands of aussies are reportedly accessing foreign content via virtual private network ( vpn ) technology , which alters their ip address to bluff their way around geographic restrictions . hbo has warned internet users , who are watching popular tv shows such as games of thrones , the sopranos and girls , via email that they will deactivate unsuspected accounts on april 21 .
