football shirt friday got into full swing as celebrities showed off their favourite kits in support of the bobby moore fund for cancer research uk . fans were asked to unite against bowel cancer by donning a football shirt – old or new – in memory of 1966 world cup-winning captain bobby moore . moore died from bowel and liver cancer on february 4 , 1993 , at the age of 51 , and bianca westwood , rob lee and clare balding were among those showing their support on social media .
