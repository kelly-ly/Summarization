eating fast food after a workout may be just as beneficial as dietary supplements , a study has claimed . performance remained the same after resting for several hours , while levels of glycogen - used by muscles as ‘fuel’ - were actually slightly higher after fast food was eaten as a recovery food . however , the researchers said that the experiment included a fairly small sample , and the long-term effects on workout recovery of eating fast food over other , healthier sources of food are not known .
