the dark lords of the sith 's heavy-breathing sound was devised by american sound engineer ben burtt in the seventies . its creator ben burtt says it makes vader sound like a ' life support system ' , ' mechanical ' , and ' part robot ' . he says that what started out as a very loud and noise-producing project which made vader sound like a ' moving operating room ' and ' queezing windmill ' , was eventually toned down to the ' life support system ' , ' mechanical ' , ' part robot ' sound we know today .
