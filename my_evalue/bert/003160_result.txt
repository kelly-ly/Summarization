yoko ono has led the tributes to john lennon 's first wife cynthia calling her a ' wonderful mother ' with a ' strong zest for life ' after she died at the age of 75 following a short battle with cancer . cynthia met lennon at art school in liverpool in 1957 and the couple married just before beatlemania transformed her husband from a jobbing musician into one of the most famous men in the world . in later years , cynthia wrote in her book of how she was mistreated by lennon as he soared to fame . cynthia was often left to look after the couple 's son julian ( pictured together in 2011 ) at home by herself as lennon traversed the globe with the beatles .
