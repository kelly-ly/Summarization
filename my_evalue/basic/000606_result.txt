( cnn ) the listeria outbreak that prompted blue bell creameries to recall their entire product line dates to 2010 , according to the centers for disease control . after weeks of gradual recalls , the company recalled all its ice cream , frozen yogurt , sherbet and other frozen treats sold in 23 states because they could be contaminated with listeria monocytogenes , the company said monday . three people in kansas have died in the past year and 10 people in four states have fallen ill from the bacteria believed to have come from blue bell products , the cdc said tuesday .
