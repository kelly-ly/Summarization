dikembe mutombo , an eight-times nba all-star who was famous for swatting away opponents ' shots and wagging his finger at them , was elected to the naismith memorial basketball hall of fame on monday . mutombo , a shot-blocking congolese centre whose legendary finger-wagging motion became one of the most recognized gestures in the game , was a four-times nba defensive player of the year and led the league in blocked shots for five straight seasons . mutombo played in the nba from 1991 until 2009 , recording 11,729 points , 12,359 rebounds , and 3,289 blocks during 18 seasons spent mostly with denver , atlanta and houston .
