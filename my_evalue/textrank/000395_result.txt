the vatican says pope francis may add another leg to his trip to the united states this september , visiting cuba just months after he helped negotiate a diplomatic thaw between the two nations . francis , the first pontiff to hail from latin america , played a key role in the re-establishment of diplomatic relations between cuba and the united states , earning praise from both president barack obama and cuban president raul castro . the pope made personal pleas to obama and cuban leaders in private letters , writing that the two nations should try to reset their relations after decades of friction .
